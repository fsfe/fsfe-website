FTF: Services

Services

Administrative Support

We can help answer questions about Free Software license adoption or use. Our services in this area range from describing the type of formal structures that can make using code easier through to assisting with community relations or resolving issues with incompliant code. We can also help you find legal and technical experts to provide specialised services when necessary.

Fiduciary License Agreement (FLA)

We make a specialised copyright assignment document called the Fiduciary License Agreement available to the community at large. The FLA allows one entity to safeguard all of the code created for a project by consolidating copyright (or exclusive exploitation rights). We can assist projects or business adapt and adopt it for their own use.

Fiduciary Programme

We also use the FLA for our own Fiduciary Programme. This allows projects to assign copyright to FSFE, and FSFE then deals with any legal issues that occur for that project. This frees up developers to focus on coding great new solutions.

Training and Consultancy

We provide extensive services in the field of Free Software education and deployment. Whether you want to explore this form of licensing for the first time, or simply optimise your current use of the licenses, we have a solution available. We also specialise in helping individuals and projects use Free Software more effectively.

Public Speaking

We speak on topics ranging from Free Software licensing through to code governance, business use cases, issue resolution and project management. We are flexible with regards audience size and demographic, and deliver speeches both commercial and as part of FSFE's educational outreach activities.

Documentation and Links

We provide documentation and links that help contextualise the expectations and obligations involved in using Free Software. We can also assist in researching what material might be useful for your individual requirements.

License Compliance

We work with our partners at gpl-violations.org to resolve licensing issues that affect both commercial and non-commercial projects or products. Our activity in this sphere is purely educational and designed to help strengthen the long-term value proposition of Free Software.

Learn More

For more information about any of our services simply contact us. We welcome both commercial and non-commercial enquiries, and are glad to assist individuals as well as projects or businesses.
