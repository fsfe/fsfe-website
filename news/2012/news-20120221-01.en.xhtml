Thank you for celebrating #ilovefs the whole day without borders

Our #ilovefs-campaign was granted around the globe and people were celebrating February 14th as the "I love Free Software" Day. Thank you very much for participating and showing your support for Free Software on this day - we were very excited about your creativity and participiation. And all these love declarations did not went out specifically to us but to the developers behind every Free Software product and their community. As a lot of you dedicated your love to specific programs that you enjoy so much, these developers will have read your love declaration, too. And for sure, they were just excited as we are. Here are just a few examples and highlights of your love declarations:
