Conseils et formation

Formation :

Nous faisons des cours de formation en Europe couvrant les bases du logiciel libre jusqu'aux interactions complexes entre communautés et la gouvernance. Nous dispensons cette formation à la fois aux entités commerciales et non-commerciales. Il suffit de nous contacter pour discuter d'une solution adaptée à vos besoins et votre budget.

Conseil :

Nous fournissons également des services de conseil commerciaux dans nos bureaux ou au lieu de votre choix, à des taux compétitifs. Notre équipe de conseil couvre davantage que simplement la pédagogie. Nous vous aidons à manager vos projets, développer des processus en interne et communiquer avec la communauté. Pour en savoir plus, contactez-nous.

Selecting a License

License selection for a Free Software project or product can be one of the most important decisions you make: the license influences how the community grows, how contributions can be received and which business models make sense around the software.

The FTF will advise you on license selection strategies and examine the way you want to work in order to pick the most applicable license for your software.

Typical license consulting will take about four hours, depending on the complexity of the project and businesses around it. Regular consulting rates apply. On-site consulting is available as well.

Consulting Rates

Consulting by members of the FTF is at a rate of 150 EUR per hour from any of our offices, or 100 EUR per hour for companies with Fellows of the FSFE as employees.

On-site consulting is available as well, for standard rates plus travel, accomodation and out-of-pocket expenses.

Workshops

The FTF offers workshops on a wide range of topics. Whether you are just starting to engage with the Free Software world, or whether you are in need of advanced supply chain licensing information, the FTF can provide in-house or external workshops to help you move your business forward.
