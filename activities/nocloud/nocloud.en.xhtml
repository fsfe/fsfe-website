No Cloud

Many people talk about "the cloud" nowadays, using that word to indicate all kinds of different things. But what is the cloud? Answering to that question is hard, and yet we put everything in "the cloud": music, videos, documents, emails, programs, ... Who can say where, exactly, are your data? Who owns them?

For a long time we have been asking people: who controls the technology you use? With the "no cloud" graphics, we ask: who controls the technology that controls your data? Who, ultimately, controls your data?

If you want other people to start thinking about these issues, you may use our promotional material. You can order and download stickers, postcards and posters, for free, from our "Spread The Word" page or buy a bag in our shop.
