European Elections project

Templates to use

From 22 to 25 May 2014, people all across the European Union are asked to vote for European Parliament for the next 5 years. This is a good time to ask the candidates about their support of Free Software. It is very easy to participate, we offer a pact you can use, templates and we take care of public awareness.

Templates of the written pact

You can either download and print the pact to send it to candidates in a letter - or download and attach it to an email to the candidates. You find email-templates below.
