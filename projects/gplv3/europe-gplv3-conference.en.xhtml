The conference

Venue: Aula 1, Centre de Cultura Contemporània de Barcelona (CCCB). Centrally located, it is near Plaça Catalunya and Les Rambles. CCCB have a travel information page, and also a map of the local area.

The international GPLv3 conferences aim to raise global awareness of the public process for drafting version three of the GNU General Public License: GPLv3.

As the details such as the schedule and practical information for people travelling, are finalised, they will be published on this page.

Although a wholly seperate event, we are glad to point out that GUADEC 2006 will take from June 24th to 30th, also in in Barcelona. GUADEC is an annual conference about GNOME - the GNU project's desktop. For information, see the GUADEC 2006 website.
