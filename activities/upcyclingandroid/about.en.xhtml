1 Upcycling Android

Keep using your phone with Free Software!

Do you find yourself changing your smartphone device too often?

Are you aware of the impact that growing smartphone production has on our climate?

Then keep using your phone with Free Software!

E-waste increases every year. The production of devices requires a lot of energy and resources. To save the planet from being overburdened we need to deal with technology in a more sustainable way. Let us start with one of the most vital pieces of technology in our daily life: smartphones are too valuable devices, to be replaced every other year or even less.

Even when the software lags, hardware remains in good condition.

You may decide to change your device for many reasons. The device becomes slow or non-responsive, or the manufacturing company stops supporting its software, which leads to security risks. In most of these cases the hardware still works pretty well. So why tossing away your shiny expensive hardware? Wouldn't it be better instead to update and keep using it? That is what Upcycling Android is about!

The software can be replaced.

Smartphones can be modified. An operating system is a collection of software that enables you to use your phone and run applications on it. You do not need to stick to the default operating system. Instead, you can install an alternative operating system on your phone.

Your device can perform better with Free Software.

How many apps were already on your phone the first time you turned it on? How many of them are you actually using? How many of them run advertisements you are not interested in? Unwanted software and trackers often exhausts the battery and processing power of your device. Switch to Free Software and take back control of your apps. It is your device! Only install the apps you like and you will see your phone performing better than before.

What you can do to make it yours

Smartphones with alternative and Free Software operating systems give you full control over your device. This way you can modify and update the software as long as you wish and need.

Reclaim your smartphone

Replace the operating system of your smartphone with a Free Software operating system. Often you need to root your phone to do so. The vast majority of smartphones can be rooted. There are plenty of Free Software operating systems available which are called "custom ROMs". Each custom ROM comes with different focus, some focus on privacy, others on freedom, on usability and more. Choose the one that matches your purpose best. Your phone will be updated, upgraded and upcycled! Enjoy the look and the feel of a brand new device.

Install Free Software apps

Even if you are still using your stock Android: Installing Free Software apps is possible in any type of smartphone. You can simply install F-Droid and immediately enjoy the biggest Free Software app store in the Android world! Browsers, Maps, Games, Music, Chats and more, everything in one place! Every app you replace with a Free Software app gets you closer to a long-lasting enjoyable device. Maximise the use of Free Software to contribute to a sustainable digital environment.

Join the movement

Free Software is everywhere around! Millions of people use, share, and develop it everyday. Communities surround the most popular apps and a helping attitude often welcomes newbies. Join us in promoting Free Software into the Android world!

Spread the word

Help us get the message out! We have prepared information material for you to share with your friends, to put up in a community space or share it in the Internet. Let people know you are upcycling your phone and why they should better do as well!

Do it yourself

You can find here everything you need to know in a nutshell about how to upcycle your device on our Upcycling Android pages. Find recommendations for operating systems and Free Software apps to get started. Upcycling your phone might seem complex in the beginning but can become really fun pretty soon.

And if you are already an expert in installing custom ROMs, you are most welcome to help by sharing your skills with others. Feel free to contact us for this or any other purpose.
