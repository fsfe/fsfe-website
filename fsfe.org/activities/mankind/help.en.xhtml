1 How to help the "Free Software as a World Cultural Heritage" project

How to help?

  • talk about the project and spread the word
  • take part to discussions (see Contact)
  • get support from other associations (see Supports)
  • contact your national commission for UNESCO (belongs to Foreign Affaires in France for example) to make them aware
  • translate project pages (.xhtml) in others languages
  • keep us in touch with publications related to the project
