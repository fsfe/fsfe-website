Documents

  • January 3rd, 2005: Open letter to Mr. Lee, General Secretary of the World Health Organisation (WHO) by Georg Greve: "Software patents are putting the wealth of the software patent lobby over the health of citizens in Europe and around the world".

  • December 6th, 2004: Open letter to Mr. Haider, President of the Union of the Electricity Industry - EURELECTRIC by Georg Greve: "Software patents detrimental to European power supply business".

  • November 1st, 2004: Open letter to Mr. Barroso by Georg Greve: "A compulsory insurance against software patents is like firefighting with petrol".

  • October 4th, 2004: Open letter to Mr. Diekmann, CEO of Allianz Group by Georg Greve: "Allianz Group will suffer because of software patents".

  • September 6th, 2004: Open letter to the Dutch Prime Minister Dr. Balkenende by Georg Greve: "Europe should conserve a competitive advantage and prohibit a legal basis for software patents".

  • August 2nd, 2004: Open letter to Dr. Wladawsky-Berger of IBM by Stefano Maffulli and Georg Greve: "Software patents will destroy the foundations of IBM's future".

  • July 6th, 2004: Open letter to Prof. Bullinger of Fraunhofer Gesellschaft, (FhG) because of software patents by Georg Greve.

  • May 31st, 2004: Open letter to Prof. Köhler, designated German President, on software patents by Georg Greve.

  • May 10th, 2004: Open letter to all citizens of Europe by Georg Greve and Hartmut Pilch.

Further Information

You can find detailed information on various issues related to software patents on the web page of our associate organisation, the FFII at http://swpat.ffii.org.
