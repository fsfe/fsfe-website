Information for Editors

The editors team is responsible for editing and proofreading material content published by FSFE. New articles are requested which the team provide, and other teams; particularly the web team, make extensive use of the editor's skills. Contribute your command of English to the Free Software movement by joining our editorial team.

  • The editors' mailing list is where the team communicates and makes decisions. Sign up to see what projects are currently underway.
  • The editor's workflow tracker is used to organise all editorial tasks. New and existing items in need of editing are listed here, and can be accepted as an assignment by any volunteer.
  • The editorial guidelines are used to maintain a consistent, high standard of textual content. Read through them to understand our aims and principles when communicating in writing.
