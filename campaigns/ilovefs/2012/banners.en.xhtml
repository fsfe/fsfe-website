Here you can find banners for our online campaign "I love Free Software" held on Valentine's Day, 14th of February 2012. Your support is high appreciated! Thank you very much in advance!

Listed below you find a selection of banners we made to show your support for Free Software. These banners listed here come together with code snippets to easily implement it on your homepage or blog. Please be aware, that this is just a selction. We made a lot of more banners, so please feel free to browse a bunch of diverse banners in different sizes and styles to implement it the way you like to ... all these banners are expressing the message "I love Free Software" in its own way and design. Hope you find your favorite, too!

We also have the graphics available in scalable SVG format if you need to make adjustments. We welcome translations of these graphics: Please send your finished translations to translators@fsfeurope.org to have them published here.
