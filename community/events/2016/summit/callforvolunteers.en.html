Call for Volunteers

We are in search for motivated volunteers, make your mark and volunteer for the FSFE Summit!

You will be an integral part of the summit and help to create a friendly and memorable conference experience for hundreds of participants from all over Europe and the world. As a volunteer you will help us with one of the most important stuffs in this Summit. Aside from your scholarship, you will also get to enjoy the provided meals throughout the event.

Some of the volunteer tasks include:

  • Moderating a talk: You will be required to moderate or facilitate panel discussions and talks. Opening and closing statements to introduce and conclude the talk will be needed. Arriving 15 minutes prior to each session to help all presenters put their presentation files on the PC using a USB thumb drive or CD/DVD if they haven't done it yet and help clip on and activate the microphone for each speaker.
  • Conference Room Facilitators: You will be required to assist with room counts, facilitate with participants entering/exiting conference rooms.
  • Information Support: directions between conference locations, runners, and greeters. You'll be required to answer the same questions over and over again. Typical questions are for telephone numbers of certain crew-members, the location of workshop rooms, and so on.

Most on-site volunteers are needed for various shifts in the hours from 9AM-6PM, September 2-4, 2016. Volunteers are typically able to attend over half of the conference sessions or more depending on the assignments of certain positions such as Room Monitors or Info support.

Here are some instructions to further guide you:

  • Step 1: Send us an email
  • Step 2: Wait for the acceptance letter sent to you
  • Step 3: Respond that you accept the assignment
  • Step 4: Register for the conference

You can apply here and take advantage of the scholarship of up to 300 Euros!
