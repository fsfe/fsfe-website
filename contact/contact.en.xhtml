We can assist you in many issues related to Free Software, and we provide many services to the community and businesses. We maintain the world's largest private network of legal experts on Free Software; we offer professional services to businesses with interests in Free Software; and we work with policy issues and campaigns in favour of open standardisation and Free Software.
