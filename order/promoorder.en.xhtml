FSFE’s Promotion Material

Are you organising a Free Software meeting or even a Fellowship evening? You like to promote Free Software and the FSFE? Then we'll be happy to send you our promotion material, of which you find an overview on this page. You might either order our default promotion package or order individual quantities. Material and shipping is for free, please find all details below.

Why and when to order

There are various reasons why and when you might like to receive promotion material for Free Software and the FSFE. Maybe you know a location (e.g. a Hackerspace, a youth club, your office...) where you would love to see information about our mission? Or you would like to tell someone (e.g. your friends, your family, your colleagues...) about Free Software, the FSFE and what we are doing? Or you are even planning a public activity (e.g. a booth, a meeting, a conference...) to promote FSFE and its goals? These are just some examples for the various usage of our promotion material. Please, feel free to decide for yourself when, why and for what you would like to use it.

How and what to order

To order promotion material please use the following order form. You might either order our default package or tell us explicitly what kind of promotion material you need or prefer. For example, if you plan to support a special FSFE`s campaign. Please keep in mind, that for postal reasons, in general we just send packages up to 1kg. If you need more, please ask.

Available promotion material

About FSFE and the Fellowship

>Our FSFE folder. On the inside of these folders, there is written some core information about FSFE, Free Software and how to contribute. They are available in an English and a German version. These folders are made to handle additional paper with more information inside, they are available in size A4 and in size A5. Inside of the latter, we normally put our political leaflets and/or our Fellowship leaflets (see next item). The others, the ones in size A4, can be useful in other contexts, for example to spread informations to the attendees of a conference.

Our Fellowship leaflets. These leaflets are in size A5, so they fit perfectly into our FSFE folder (see item above). It is a nice piece of a graphical artwork, explaining why someone should think about becoming a fellow. The print is double sided: One side is in English, the other one in German.

The classic Plussy stickers, the symbol of FSFE`s Fellowship. The background is transparent, so the sticker integrates perfectly in any sourrounding - not just on laptops. Size: 7 x 7cm.

Our "Join the Fellowship" poster. A good poster to pin on a prominent place, for example inside a meeting room or a Hackerspace and for use at any public activity. Available in size A1.

Free Software

This flyer is about Free Software and the GNU project and compares Free Software to the use of screws and screwdrivers. On the last page then, it promotes Richard Stallman and the FSFE and the Fellowship of the FSFE. Attention: This flyer is only available in German right now but is in progress of translation and production for other languages, too.

Digital Restrictions Management

This is a leaflet about Digital Restriction Management and its dangers. We produced the leaflet together with Digitale Gesellschaft and it is a good summary of this complex theme. It is size A5 and has 4 pages. Language is German.

"Free Your Android" campaign

This is the poster of our "Free Your Android" campaign for which there are flyers available, too. So far, experience shows that this poster is very eye catchy and attracts a lot of people to ask about the backgrounds of this campaign. Hence, we recommend to use it at any booth. Size A1.

This is the flyer of our "Free Your Android" campaign for which are posters available, too. They are in size A7, that makes them very easy to spread and for interested persons very easy to put in pocket or their moneybag. The print is double sided: One side is in English, the other one in German.

"I love Free Software" campaign

This is the flyer of our I love Free Software campaign. The flyer has two sides. One side with the IloveFS-heart, same as in the poster. The other side telling the date and the URL - as you can see in this image. Size A7.

This is the poster of our I love Free Software campaign. The poster comes as you see in the image and is very attractive in its colors and its design. Size A1.

"Document Freedom Day" campaign

If you are looking for DFD material in particular, please have a look at our promotion order page on documentfreedom.org. If you like to mix other promotion material with promotion material from the DFD campaign, feel free to order it here.

The "I cannot read your documents" stickers address our Document Freedom Day campaign. The slogan highlights the problem in the use of proprietary file formats, as they exclude all people not using the same proprietary software from reading the document. Size: 7,5 x 7,5cm.

This is the poster of Document Freedom Day campaign 2013. It's timeless and the poster can be used stand-alone or you can use it to announce a DFD-event. Format is A2.

This is the poster of Document Freedom Day campaign 2012. It's timeless and the poster includes a nice quote from Stephen Fry. Format is A2.

"PDFreaders" campaign

This is the poster of our PDFreaders campaign. The poster is plain, so you have to fill out the actual numbers of organisations and individuals signing our petition by yourself. This is long lasting campaign now, still people like it very much. Size A1.
