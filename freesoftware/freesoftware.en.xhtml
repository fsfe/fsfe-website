Introduction to Free Software

Free Software is all about freedom and community. These sections introduce the role of Free Software in key areas of society. At the end of each section you'll find more information about what the Free Software Foundation Europe is doing in that field, and how you can help.

Statistics

The Free Software movement was started in 1984 in the United States. Since then it has been growing and expanding, so that it is now present on the 5 continents and has penetrated all the spheres of our society. If you want to see some figures, have a look at the statistics' page.
