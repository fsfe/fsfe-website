Fellowship Grant

The Fellowship grant was a way for people involved in Free Software activism to become Fellows, even if they could not afford the Fellowship donation. Since now all volunteers can join the Fellowship without the need of any payment, it has become obsolete.

There are various ways of contributing to FSFE, where financial support is only one of the options. No matter in which way you choose to get involved in FSFE â€“ you are welcome to the Fellowship!

When you are involved in any volunteer activity within FSFE, please get in contact with the coordinator of your group, who will give you instructions how to join the Fellowship.
