Libre Software Meeting - Photos from some FSF related people

Photos taken during the second Libre Software Meeting (Bordeaux, France), from July, 4th 2001 to July, 8th 2001.

  • Bradley Khun, Vice President of the FSF
  • Jaime Villate, Ruben Leote Mendes and Hugo Nogueira (members of the FSFE-Portugal mailing list, list of open discussions about FSF Europe and Portugal) who took the first steps toward the goal of forming a Free Software dedicated organization in Portugal.
  • Leandro Noferini who is a founding member of the ASSOLI organization in Florence, Italy. ASSOLI was created by 16 members from all over Italy.
  • Wolfgang Sourdeau from GNU Philosophy translator team and Francis Lacoste who are in charge of creating the FSF Canada together with Richard M. Stallman.
  • Eben Moglen, Professor of Law and Legal History at the Columbia Law School and General Counsel for the FSF
  • Werner Koch, from GnuPG, and member of the FSF Europe
  • Mélanie Clement-Fontaine, Phd student in Intellectual Property Rights, author of a study of the GNU GPL and responsible for the translation in french of GNU GPL project for the FSFE France.
  • Cyril Bouthors and Igor Genibel who are be actively involved in FSFE France activities thru Savannah.
  • Tony Bassette, Olivier Berger, Benjamin Drieu members of the Administrative Council of APRIL
  • Loïc Dachary, Vice President of the FSF Europe
  • Frédéric Couchet, president of the FSFE France
