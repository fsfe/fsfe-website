FSFE Legal Activities

FSFE is active in Free Software legal issues at at every level from the national to the UN.

European Legal Network

We facilitate the European Legal Network (ELN), a private network of legal professionals with over 240 members across 27 countries and four continents. Members share knowledge to increase the availability of best practice information and understanding in Free Software licensing.

Conference

We host the 'European Legal Network Confernece' to discuss the current market situation and projected future for Free Software licenses. This is the premier event for debating, discussing and sharing knowledge around Free Software legal affairs.

Special Interest Groups

We organize Special Interest Groups to discuss topics like business processes, license management and trademarks. These groups are designed to reduce misunderstandings, share knowledge and address areas where there is a lack of available guidance.
