Free Software in Portugal

On October 12, the city of Porto hosted the second edition of Porto Cidade Tecnológica 2001, an event dedicated to Free Software in Portugal. In an old market slightly above the river, a conference room was installed in the center. Booth, cyber cafe and install parties surrounded it. The city of Porto provided the organisational infrastructure and many people supported the initiative.
