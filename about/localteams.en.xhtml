Local FSFE Groups

The Free Software Foundation is a non-profit association that started to be built by volunteers. Today, the FSFE has some paid staff to make daily operation smooth and possible. Still, FSFE's work would not be possible without the help of volunteers and the backening of the community. Many community and team members cooperate via Internet or other media, while others prefer to meet people in real life. That's how local FSFE meetings have been established.
If you are looking for contact, if you are looking for information or if you like to join a group, you can find your local contact partner on this page.
