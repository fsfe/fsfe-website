Free Software in the European Union

The FSFE actively promote the use of Free Software at the European level. European Institutions and member state's public bodies should already use and distribute Free Software which are the best way to fit with their obligations of transparency, non-discrimation and interoperability.

In order to defend efficiently Free Software in Europe we have defined global policy goals which are our guiding principles in the activities we lead.

Navigation

  • Policy Goals

    For the 2014-2019 period, we chose to focus on four important current thematics:

    • Free Software in public bodies
    • Consumer rights and device sovereignty
    • Privacy, surveillance and cryptography
    • Distributed systems

  • Activities

    To participate in the policy making process of the European Union, to create a summary of European legislations, to be part of some important cases,... are examples of actions we are doing or have done in Europe.
