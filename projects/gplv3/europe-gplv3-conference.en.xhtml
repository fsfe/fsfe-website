The 3rd International GPLv3 Conference:
CCCB, Barcelona, Spain, June 22nd & 23rd

Registration is mandatory. To register, email oriordan@fsfeurope.org and put the exact phrase "GPLv3 registration" in the subject of the email. Please put only your name, country, and affiliation in the body. This may be printed on name badges or used for planning discussion groups. You will receive a reply within 24 hours.

For more information about GPLv3 and how to participate, see our GPLv3 project page.

The conference

Venue: Aula 1, Centre de Cultura Contemporània de Barcelona (CCCB). Centrally located, it is near Plaça Catalunya and Les Rambles. CCCB have a travel information page, and also a map of the local area.

The international GPLv3 conferences aim to raise global awareness of the public process for drafting version three of the GNU General Public License: GPLv3.

There will be simultaneous translation from English into Spanish for the whole conference. There will also be Spanish to English translation during the software patents talk on the second day. All other presentations will be made in English.

Although a wholly separate event, we are glad to point out that GUADEC 2006 will take from June 24th to 30th, also in Catalonia. GUADEC is an annual conference about GNOME - the GNU project's desktop. For information, see the GUADEC 2006 website.
