Hardware Donations

The Free Software Foundation Europe would like to thank all donors of equipment for their support which made it possible for us to continue working for Free Software.

If you are interested to donate equipment, please write an email to office@fsfeurope.org in which you describe the kind of equipment you want to donate. You can also support us by donating money or get active yourself.

If you are looking for the sponsoring buttons, they can be found here.

  • 2005-01-17: Hewlett-Packard Germany donated two Compaq N2400 servers (Dual CPU, Pentium III 1GHz with 1GB RAM each) which we are using as web- and mailserver.
