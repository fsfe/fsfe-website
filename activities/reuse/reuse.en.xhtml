REUSE of Free Software

By lowering the barrier to reuse Free Software, we can entrench the position of Free Software as the foundation and default for all new software.

FSFE is elaborating and promoting best practices for Free Software developers to encourage reuse of their software. Based on existing sources and the experience of our community, we want to show and encourage the practices we know make the process of reuse easier.

The project is actively collecting sources of best practices from various writings, groups and organisations, from which we'll extract relevant practices and collate them into a shortlist of the most important ones.

Come work with us on our WeKan board!
