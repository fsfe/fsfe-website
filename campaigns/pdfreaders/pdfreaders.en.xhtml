Free Software PDF readers

The public administration should not advertise non-free software on their websites. Nevertheless they do so, especially in the field of PDF software as you can see in the screenshots below, where a lot of pages encourage visitors to download non-free PDF readers. The goal of the campaign is to find out how much non-free software advertisement is done on government websites and call their attention to this. When they make advertisement, they should advertise Free Software PDF readers because it allows people to be independent of a single vendor.

How to participate

From the 13th of September to the 17th October we ask everybody to search for such advertising on governmental websites and to report this to us using the formular underneath. It also allows you to sign our petition for governmental websites without non-free software advertisement.

The three best individual hunters will win a free entry to FSCONS, the world's premier conference for social change in technology taking place in Gothenburg from November 5th to November 7th 2010. Additionnaly, the winning team and the individual winners will recieve a special FSFE present box at the end of October.

Thank you for contributing with the FSFE to set governmental websites without non-free software advertisement !
