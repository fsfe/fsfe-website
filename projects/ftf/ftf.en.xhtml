Freedom Task Force (FTF)

Overview

The Freedom Task Force collects and shares knowledge about Free Software law, safeguards the interests of Free Software projects, connects experts throughout the world and helps other groups achieve similar goals. We do this in partnership with gpl-violations.org and members of the European Legal Network.

We provide education and consultancy services at competitive market rates. You can contact us 24 hours a day by email or during normal German working hours by telephone with questions or requests for assistance.

Documentation

The FTF keeps a collection of documentation on Free Software Legal and Licensing topics. It also writes its own guides and handbooks.

European Legal Network

The FTF maintains the world's largest private, non-partisan, vendor-neutral network of legal experts in the field of Free Software licensing. This network is active in developing knowledge on licensing and legal issues, interprets and analyses licenses and thinks about the answers to the next Big Question around Free Software and legal.

The European Legal Network (ELN) is a world-wide network and is actively looking for legal experts in Central and Eastern Europe, the Far East and Latin America. If you are general counsel for a Free Software company, head of a Free-Software-oriented legal department, academic researcher or legal leader for a Free Software project, please contact us.

Each year, the ELN Workshop is held to help spread knowledge among lawyers and counsel on topics at the forefront of governance for Free Software projects. In 2010, the workshop will be held in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. You can find the conference information here.

For Business

Business increasingly uses Free Software and engages with Free Software development. This leads to questions about licensing, the legal structure of Free Software projects, mechanisms for engaging with Free Software projects and means of governing your own projects. The FTF can provide advice on these topics through workshops, training or presentations. We regularly speak at conferences on these and similar topics; these presentations can be given at your business as well.
