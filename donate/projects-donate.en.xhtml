What happens next

If you donate more than 120 Euro in a year, we would like to add your name to our donors list. For online donations, you can use the checkbox "I want to remain anonymous" to indicate whether or not being listed there is ok with you. If you send your donation by bank transfer, please contact us about this.

Additionally, you can include our Sponsoring buttons on your own web page to show your support for us.

In some countries, donations to us can be deducted from income tax. Currently, this is the case in Germany, the Netherlands, and Switzerland.
