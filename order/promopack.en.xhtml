FSFE`s Promotion Material

This is an overview of FSFE's promotion material for various usage. To order material, please contact us directly and explain what kind of material you would like to receive. Material and shipping is for free. Please find all details below.

Why and when to order

There are various reasons why and when you should receive promotion material from the FSFE. For example you are a Fellowship group coordinator and you are planning to set up a public activity to promote FSFE and our work. Or you would like to tell your friends or colleagues about FSFE and its activities. Or you know a place (e.g. a Hackerspace, a youth club...) where there should be some advertisement for FSFE. Feel free to decide for yourself when and why it is useful for you to receive our promotion material.

How and what to order

To order, please write us an email including your order and your snail-mail address. You might either order our default package or tell us explicitly what kind of promotion material you need or prefer. For example, if you plan to support a special FSFE`s campaign. Please keep in mind, that for postal reasons, in general we send packages up to 1kg. If you need more, please ask.

Our default 1kg-package consists of:

  • 3 FreeYourAndroid-Poster, 2 Join the Fellowship-Poster, 1 PDFreaders-Poster
  • 4 cm FreeYourAndroid-Flyer
  • 25 Fellowship-leaflets
  • some Stickers: "I cannot read your documents", GNU-Sticker, Plussy-Sticker ...
