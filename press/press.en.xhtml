Press Contact

If you are a member of the press, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us. To get into contact with a specific person or function, please take a look at our contact section.

For general contact (in English), please use press@fsfeurope.org

For France-specific things (in French or English), please use press@france.fsfeurope.org

For Germany-specific things (in German or English), please use press@germany.fsfeurope.org

To obtain press-releases, you can subscribe to the following mailing lists:

  • press-release (for English press releases) ( web interface, archive)

  • press-release-fr (for French press releases) ( web interface, archive)

  • press-release-de (for German press releases) ( web interface, archive)

  • press-release-it (for Italian press releases) ( web interface, archive)
