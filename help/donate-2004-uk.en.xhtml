Donations to the FSF Europe in the United Kingdom

Due to substantial bank fees charged for international money transfers, small donations or standing orders are too expensive to be send directly to the FSF Europe bank account. To rectify this, we entered into an agreement with our UK based associate organisation AFFS to collect donations for us and transfer them in larger batches.
