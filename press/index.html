Press Contact

If you are a member of the press, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us. To get into contact with a specific person or function, please take a look at our contact section.

For general contact you can use one of the following email adresses:

  • general contact (in English): press@fsfeurope.org
  • for France specific things (in English or French): press@france.fsfeurope.org (also points to france@press.fsfeurope.org)
  • for Germany specific things (in English or German): press@germany.fsfeurope.org (also points to germany@press.fsfeurope.org)

Press Release Mailing Lists

There are three mailing lists used to publish press releases. These mailing lists are post-only by the FSFE and their subscriber lists are non public.

  • press-release (for English press releases) (web interface, archive)
  • press-release-fr (for French press releases) (web interface, archive)
  • press-release-de (for German press releases) (web interface, archive)

To access old press releases please follow the links to the mailing list archives.
