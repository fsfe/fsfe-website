Freedom Task Force

The Freedom Task Force provides licence support services to individuals, projects and businesses involved with Free Software. We work in partnership with gpl-violations.org to strengthen the legal foundation of the Free Software eco-system.

Contact us with a question...

Our Services

  • Licensing education
  • Fiduciary services
  • Licence enforcement

Our Methods

The Freedom Task Force has a full-time coordinator and a network of legal and technical volunteers. On demand, we will form ad-hoc teams of experts to deal with specific issues. We believe in positive engagement and constructive dialogue. We seek to resolve issues amicably and for the long-term benefit of Free Software.

Our Goals

  • To collect, analyse and share legal knowledge about Free Software.
  • To safeguard the interests of Free Software projects and businesses.
  • To bring together experts in Free Software legal issues.
  • To work with other groups and help them achieve similar goals.
  • Learn more through our FAQ.
