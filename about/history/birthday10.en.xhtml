Excerpts of FSFE's history

  • 2000-11-22 - "Declaration of Intent - Free Software Foundation Europe": at that time a group of four people Bernhard Reiter, Georg C. F. Greve, Peter Gerwinski, and Werner Koch asked others to join the team to create a European Free Software organisation.
  • 2001-02-22 - "Current status": The group now consisted of members from France, Germany, Italy, and Sweden.
  • 2001-03-31: First FSFE Website
  • 2001-04-24 - "Free Software Foundation Europe finishes founding process" announcing that FSFE "has officially begun activities on March 10th, 2001".
  • 2006-03-10: FSFE's 5th birthday
  • 2009-03-10: Fellowship interview with founding president Georg Greve about FSFE's history
  • 2009-03-11: FSFE's 8th birthday
  • 2010-01-26: FSFE honoured with Theodor Heuss Medal - "trendsetting organisation"
  • 2010-04-28: FSFE founder Georg Greve awarded German Cross of Merit
