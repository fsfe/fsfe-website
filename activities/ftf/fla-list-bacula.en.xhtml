Fiduciary Legal Agreement - FSFE & Bacula Project

Since 2006 developers of the Bacula Project has signed Fiduciary Licence Agreements with FSFE. The purpose of this agreement has been to ensure the lasting protection of Free Software by making FSFE the fiduciary of the author's interests. It empowers FSFE - and its sister organisations - to uphold the interests of Free Software interests and protect them in court if necessary.
