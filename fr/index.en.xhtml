Welcome to the web site of the Free Software Foundation France, the french chapter of the Free Software Foundation Europe (FSF Europe).

FSF Europe is the sister organisation of the Free Software Foundation (the american historical organisation defending the Free Software, which started the GNU project).
FSF France is a non-profit association (governed by french law of 1901), whose object is to promote Free Software (as in "free speech") in France.
The FSF Europe works with its associate organisations. Two of these are registered in France : the Association pour la Promotion et la Recherche en Informatique Libre (APRIL - Association for Promotion and Research in Libre Computing) and Organization for Free Software in Education and Teaching (OFSET).

For more informations about the FSF, FSF Europe, the national chapters or the associate organisations, use the links in the banner on top of the page.
For information about Free Software, read the following links : What's Free Software?, and What's the GNU Project?.
