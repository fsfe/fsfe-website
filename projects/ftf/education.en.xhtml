Freedom Task Force

Licensing education

The FTF offers educational services around Free Software licensing and legal issues. We can help answer questions, train people and to provide solutions for individual requirements. We have an internal team of highly regarded legal and technical experts and if required the FTF can help you obtain formal legal advice throughout Europe.

Whether you are a project or a business we can help ensure you get the information you need to accomplish your goals.
