About FSFE's Community

The community behind the Free Software Foundation Europe is diverse and spread throughout the continent. We see ourselves as Europeans with a different cultural background but with the common vision of an infrastructure built on Free Software - as a precondition of a Free Society. This is what makes us strong. We believe in cooperation from a regional to a global level, from community to an organisational level, from freedom to liberty. You are welcome to join our community! Read this page to know more about the different ways, how to become part of our movement. then decide for yourself in which way you would like to get involved.

To join FSFE's community you can

  • Join the discussions
  • Sign up as a supporter
  • Show up at a local meeting
  • Contribute
  • Become a Fellow of the the Free Software Foundation Europe

Join the discussions

Becoming part of FSFE's community is already true for everyone who joins our discussions, gives feedback or let people know about us. Hence, if you like to do a first step towards joining our community without any obligations, join our mailing lists or other communication channels and get to know our community. Or, if you like what we do - wherever you live, wherever you go - help to spread our word. Viral marketing and spreading the word are important factors to let our movement grow.

Once you feel welcome and interested in more, we gladly invite you to drive deeper into our community by becoming a supporter, a contributor or a Fellow.

Sign up as a supporter

You can sign up as a supporter of the FSFE. As a supporter you can become our social multiplier. Therefore, you will receive our monthly newsletter as well as occasional special news about our campaigns or other important activities of the FSFE. You are also invited to use FSFE's supporter logos to show your affiliation.

Contribute

Many of the things FSFE does and is able to do, relies on the priceless help of volunteers throughout Europe. If you like engage in a community of people who are committed to Free Software, there are many ways to help us. Sure, you will find a way that fits your interests and skills. For example, we are always in need of designers, web-developers, public awareness ... and translations. Localizing is really important for our cross-border communication and outreaching activities. And please, don't mind: you do not need to be an interpreter for the translations - if you are a mother tongue speaker and you have a feeling for proper language, this is a good start.

Become a Fellow of the the Free Software Foundation Europe

'Fellows' are sustaining members of the FSFE and the backbone of our community. To become a Fellow, you can easily join the Fellowship. As a Fellow you get access to our infrastructure for you own purpose and to use it for the communication with other Fellows. You can also join or set up local teams and find help to do so by the staff of FSFE. You can even present yourself as a candidate for a Fellowship representative and become part of the General Assembly of the FSFE.

Be aware, that being a Fellow involves membership fees. However, these membership fees are financial contributions, that directly support our work and make sure that we remain independent of any particular donor. When you join the Fellowship, you can decide for yourself about the amount of your donation.
