The Freedom Task Force works to support the Free Software community, and you can help us out. While FSFE provides a full-time coordinator for the FTF, a lot of our activity is done on a volunteer basis.

Want to help? Join FSFE's discussion mailing list. You can help us generate written material to educate people regarding Free Software licensing. You can also help by spreading the word about the FTF.
