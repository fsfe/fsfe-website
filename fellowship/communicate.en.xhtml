Communicate with other Fellows

Here you find information how to communicate with other Fellows and Free Software supporters while not attending meetings by mailing lists, and chat. Beside that you can find non-Fellowship specific communication channels on our community page.

Multi User Chat

We have XMPP-Chat-Rooms where Fellows can meet:

  • Server: conference.jabber.fsfe.org
  • Channels: fellowship, rheinland, uk, de

Microblogging

  • fsfe user on Quitter.no (GNU Social), Identi.ca and Twitter
  • hashtags/groups: !fsfe !fellowship !fs !freesoftware
