About

The Free Software Foundation Europe is dedicated to the furthering of Free Software. Access to software determines who may participate in a digital society, therefore we believe the freedoms to use, copy, modify and redistribute software are essential in the information age.

Founded in 2001 as a non-profit non-governmental organisation, the FSFE focuses on creating awareness for these issues, securing Free Software politically and legally, and giving people Freedom by supporting development of Free Software.

What Free Software is

Free Software is one important cornerstone for freedom, democracy, human rights and development in a digital society. It is essential both in an economic and socio-ethical context.

How we work

As an organisation based upon the European culture and background, we believe in and are following fundamental principles such as democracy, multilateralism, cooperation, dialog and transparency. In short, we operate in a networked, team-based structure that is open to anyone sharing the common understanding of FSFE and willing to participate.
