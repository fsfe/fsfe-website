Donations to the FSFE

The Free Software Foundation Europe is a non-profit non-governmental organisation. Our work is made possible thanks to our community of volunteers and donors. Your donations are critical to our strength and autonomy — they enable us to continue fighting for Free Software wherever necessary, and to be an independent voice.

Categories

  • Donate EUR 10 every month OR EUR 120 in a year and you'll become a Supporter of the FSFE.
  • Donate EUR 50 every month OR EUR 600 in a year and you'll become a Contributor of the FSFE.
  • Donate EUR 100 every month OR EUR 1.200 in a year and you'll become a Sustaining Contributor of the FSFE.
  • Donate EUR 500 every month OR EUR 6000 in a year and you'll become a Patron of the FSFE.
