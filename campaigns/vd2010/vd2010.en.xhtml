I ♥ Free Software

This year on Valentine's Day, show your love for Free Software! Here are some suggestions for 14 February.

This time it's personal

Behind every Free Software initiative and organisation there are real people. Humans. Show them that you appreciate their work!

  • Tell them “thank you” for their work
  • Send them some chocolate
  • Buy them a drink
  • Hug a developer (ask permission first ;-)
  • Send them a book

Money can buy you love

Donate to a Free Software initiative. Organisations like FSFE depend on your contribution to continue their work. Check out your favourite organisation and make a donation. You can be sure they will love you back.

Show your love

This sticker is available in our shop.

Show the whole world that you love Free Software:

  • Put an “I love Free Software” sticker on your car, your luggage, etc.
  • Wear Free Software shirts and sweaters. If you need new stuff, you can order some from us, or from other Free Software websites.

Send us pictures of how you are showing your love for Free Software. We will publish them on this website, making a great collection of Free Software lovers from around the world!
