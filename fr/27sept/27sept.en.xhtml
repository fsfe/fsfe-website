The GNU gets adult

The FSF France association wishes to celebrate in 2001 the GNU project's majority on thursday 27th of september.

The announcement of its creation by Richard Stallman was made on 27th of september 1983. So the GNU project will be 18 years old this year.

We invite the whole community of Free Software to celebrate this event.

We will report on this page all events that take place throughout the world, and we encourage you to let us know of any event you would set up.
