Historic documents

These documents are of largely historic interest as they tend to contain a lot of information that is outdated.

  • Declaration of Intent - FSF Europe

      This was the first public announcement for the Free Software Foundation Europe, launched by the original initiators. Its goal was to inform the community about their intentions and get in touch with people from other countries who might be willing to participate. It drew an amazing amount of interest, has been featured on many news-sites and the almost empty web-site had about 15.000 hits in the first week.
