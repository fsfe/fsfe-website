General Discussion

If you are interested in general discussion about the FSFE or would like to participate, please join one of our mailing lists.

Contact

In this section we will provide you with contact information for specific individuals or functions. It also informs you about the internal structure of the FSFE.

generic email address
team@fsfeurope.org To contact everyone in the FSF Europe.
president and office
president@fsfeurope.org For contact to the President & Vice-President of the FSF Europe. Currently held by Georg C. F. Greve and Loic Dachary.
office@fsfeurope.org For contact to the office of the FSFE Europe. Current head of office is Peter Gerwinski.
FSF Europe France
chancellor@france.fsfeurope.org For contact to the Chancellor & Vice-Chancellor of France. Designated Chancellor is Frederic Couchet, Vice-Chancellor is n/a.
france@fsfeurope.org For contact to everyone involved in the public French activities of the FSF Europe. This includes the FSF Europe and associated organizations.
FSF Europe Germany
chancellor@germany.fsfeurope.org For contact to the Chancellor & Vice-Chancellor of Germany. Currently held by Bernhard Reiter and Werner Koch.
germany@fsfeurope.org For contact to everyone involved in the public German activities of the FSF Europe. This includes the FSF Europe and associated organizations.

Please Note

The President, Vice-President, Head of Office, German Chancellor and German Vice-Chancellor will be official as of Friday, 20th of April, 2001. The French Chancellor will be "designated" still.
