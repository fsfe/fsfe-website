Communicate with other Fellows

Here you find information how to communicate with other Fellows and Free Software supporters through meetings, mailing lists, and chat.

Fellowship Meetings

Active Fellowship groups are emerging in more and more European cities. These groups allow Fellows from an area to meet and undertake activities ranging from a comfortable beer to full-blown public awareness events.

Find out if a Fellowship group in your region already exists: Fellowship Groups

If no Fellowship Group exists near you, we would encourage you to start a new group. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

Jabber Multi User Chat

We have Jabber-Chat-Rooms where Fellows can meet:
