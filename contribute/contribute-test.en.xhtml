Contribute

Our work is made possible thanks to a community of contributors who wish to keep us going strong for Free Software. No matter what your skills are or how much time you have, there is always a way to help us!

Navigation

  • Join the Fellowship

    Secure our financial independence, our political weight, and help us with local activities.

  • Contribute to the website

    As webmasters and translators you ensure high quality websites.

  • Spread the word

    Spread these graphics and show that you care about Free Software.

  • Internship

    Learn how to work in a European organisation which is involved in social, political, and legal issues from a country to an UN level.

  • Join our mailing lists

    Depending on your interest participate at one of our Europe wide, country-specific, or activity-focused mailing lists.

  • Get involved with your country teams

    Contact our local country teams to get more involved in FSFE.

  • Make a donation

    This allows us to be autonomous and to keep going strong for Free Software.
