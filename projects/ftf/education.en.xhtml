We offer consultancy and education in Free Software licensing. We can answer questions, provide training and help implement best practices. We offer a variety of services for individuals, community projects and commercial businesses, using our network of legal and technical experts throughout Europe to deliver timely solutions.

Consultancy:

We can help individuals understand the requirements of Free Software licensing. We can help projects with policy, consolidating copyright or solving violation issues. We can offer businesses a full range of professional licensing consultancy services.

Our services are generally free for individuals and projects and competitively priced for businesses.

You can contact us at any time through our online form or through email, post or telephone.

Training:

We can offer individual training, training seminars or complete training courses around Free Software licensing issues. This website will shortly contain details on our training programmes. In the meantime, you can contact us to find out more.
