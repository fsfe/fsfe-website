We work in partnership with gpl-violations.org to resolve licensing issues. We seek to resolve problems for the long-term benefit of Free Software rather than regarding with each case as an isolated infringement. We seek to educate people in how to properly use Free Software.

If you wish to report or discuss a violation with us, you can do so through our online form or through email, post or telephone.

You can also visit our partner organisation gpl-violations.org for detailed information about their activities.
