About us
   In order to keep track of our actions, we try to write activity reports on a regular basis. Those reports gather all the data published on the FSF France over a period and bring them in perspective. It may not be very useful for people monitoring our activity on a daily basis. However, it allows other organizations and individuals to figure out what we do, how and why even if they can only spare one hour every three months to read those reports.
