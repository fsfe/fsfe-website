Training:

We offer training courses in Germany, Belgium, Sweden, Switzerland and The Netherlands. Our Swedish partners are Internet Academy, our Swiss partners are Digicomp academy and our Dutch partners are ATComputing. Our goal is to help ensure the provision of high quality educational courses for individuals, projects and businesses interested in Free Software.

Our Current Courses:

  • Free Software in business
  • Free Software in the public sector
  • Introduction to Free Software licensing

If you would like information about reserving a place on one of our courses or booking a course for your organisation please contact us. Our rates for individuals vary from country to country. Our rate for booking a one day course for up to 15 people is 3,500 Euro.

Consultancy:

We offer consultancy at FSFE's Zurich office at a flat rate of 150 Euro per hour, with a special rate of 100 Euro for companies with Fellowship members among their employees. The FTF also offers consultancy, in-house training and workshops at other locations on request.
