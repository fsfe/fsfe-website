About the Mailing Lists

The mailing lists are open to everyone interested in the Free Software Foundation France.

Subscription to any of the mailing lists as well as complete maintenance of subscriptions can either be done by e-mail or online with the web interface. There are also complete archives of the discussions available online.

Discussions on fsfe-france@gnu.org

fsfe-france@gnu.org is the mailing list for general discussions (in french) concerning the FSF France.

You can subscribe to fsfe-france@gnu.org by using the web interface or sending e-mail to fsfe-france-request@gnu.org with subscribe as the subject.

The archives can be found here.

News on fsfe-newsfr-en@gnu.org

fsfe-newsfr-en@gnu.org dispatches daily news about FSF France, in english, that can be consulted on the web at News.

You can subscribe to fsfe-newsfr-en@gnu.org by using the web interface or sending e-mail to fsfe-newsfr-en-request@gnu.org with subscribe as the subject.

The archives can be found here.

Press releases FSF Europe

You can subscribe to press-release@fsfeurope.org by using the web interface or sending e-mail to press-release-request@fsfeurope.org with subscribe as the subject.

The archives can be found here.

Apart from these mailing-lists specific to the french chapter, you may also subscribe to the other general mailing lists of the FSF Europe (mostly in english).
