Welcome to the web site of the Free Software Foundation France, the French chapter of the Free Software Foundation Europe (FSF Europe).

FSF Europe is the sister organization of the Free Software Foundation (the American historical organization defending Free Software, which started the GNU project).
FSF France is a non-profit association (governed by French law of 1901), whose object is to promote Free Software (as in "free speech") in France.
The FSF Europe works with its associated organizations. Two of these are registered in France: the Association pour la Promotion et la Recherche en Informatique Libre (APRIL - Association for Promotion and Research in Libre Computing) and Organization for Free Software in Education and Teaching (OFSET).

For more information about the FSF, FSF Europe, the local chapters or the associated organizations, use the links in the banner on top of the page.
For information about Free Software, follow these links: What's Free Software?, and What's the GNU Project?, or directly the philosophy section.
