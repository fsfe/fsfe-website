Job Description:

The intern will work as the only dedicated full-time office person of FSFE, as well as take the role of an assistant to FSFE's president. This involves - among many other tasks - implementation of PR activities and communication with contacts from the community, NGOs, and industry, as well as general office tasks. The intern will coordinate volunteers and FSFE team members during work on various projects. She or he will also manage the president's travel plan and occasionally accompany the president on his travels.

Requirements:

  • interest in and passion for Free Software, as well as a solid basic knowledge of the concept
  • organisation and improvisation skills
  • ability to integrate into young, motivated and professional team
  • good working knowledge of English, other languages welcome, ability to understand/speak German is a plus.
  • basic acquaintance with working on GNU/Linux systems - alternatively, you will be migrated at the start of your internship.
  • no programming skills required. Basic HTML knowledge will do. An affinity to technology and willingness to learn are indispensable, though.
  • internship duration: four months minimum up to twelve months maximum.

The Free Software Foundation Europe offers you:

  • a truly challenging and exciting time with a dynamic NGO active on an international level
  • occasion to take responsibility for your own area of work
  • close-up view of organisational and community processes
  • chance to take the initiative and put your ideas into practice
  • a chance to travel with the president to interesting places, meeting Free Software advocates across Europe

Who can apply:

Everybody fulfilling the above criterions, it doesn't matter if you are an computer science, law, economic, social or political student; we like diversity to get new ideas.

The intern will be based in FSFE's office in Hamburg, Germany. This is a full-time job. You have been warned. Please direct your application (in English) containing a letter of motivation, a CV and references, as well as any questions you might have, to:

office@fsfeurope.org

Please do not send any documents in Microsoft's Word document format.

We are looking forward to receiving your application.
