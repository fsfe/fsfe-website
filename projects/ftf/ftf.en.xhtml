FTF: Freedom Task Force

Overview

The Freedom Task Force collects and shares knowledge about Free Software law, safeguards the interests of Free Software projects, connects experts throughout the world and helps other groups achieve similar goals. We do this in partnership withgpl-violations.org and members of the European Legal Network.

The FTF has a full-time coordinator, retained counsel and an office based in Berlin, Germany. We provide commercial education and consultancy services at competitive market rates while also managing FSFE's legal affairs. You can contact us 24 hours a day by email or during normal German working hours by telephone with questions or requests for assistance.
