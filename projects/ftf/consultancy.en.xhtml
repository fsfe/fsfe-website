The Freedom Task Force offers consulting services for business and for Free Software projects on various topics. We're happy to offer some gratis advice beforehand, though.

Selecting a License

License selection for a Free Software project or product can be one of the most important decisions you make: the license influences how the community grows, how contributions can be received and which business models make sense around the software.

The FTF will advise you on license selection strategies and examine the way you want to work in order to pick the most applicable license for your software.

Typical license consulting will take about four hours, depending on the complexity of the project and businesses around it. Regular consulting rates apply. On-site consulting is available as well.

Consulting Rates

Consulting by members of the FTF is at a rate of 150 EUR per hour from any of our offices, or 100 EUR per hour for companies with Fellows of the FSFE as employees.

On-site consulting is available as well, for standard rates plus travel, accomodation and out-of-pocket expenses.
