German Chapter

The German chapter of the FSF Europe was established legally in April 2001 in Hamburg, Germany.

Members of the German chapter of the FSF Europe are:

  • Bernhard Reiter
  • Werner Koch
  • Peter Gerwinski
  • Georg C. F. Greve
  • Jonas Öberg (Sweden)

FSF Europe members from other countries had to become member in the German chapter as German law has a minimum requirement in terms of how many membery an association must have to register.
