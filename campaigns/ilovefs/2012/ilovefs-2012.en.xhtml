Valentine's Day traditionally is a day to show and celebrate your love with the one you dedicate it to. But instead of solely showing your love to your beloved one, we think Valentine's Day can be a great opportunity to declare your love and show your gratitude to Free Software, too. Or, to put it precisely, to the people behind any and every Free Software initiative or organisation. This will not only delight the people behind your preferred Free Software - beyond that, you can support the movement in celebrating and establishing Valentines Day as an "I love Free Software"-Day.
Last year, our Valentine's Day online campaign was a big success to celebrate an "I love Free Software"-Day and we like to thank you all for your support. According to last years success, we decided to keep this campaign running on an annual basis. Hence, this page is about the "I love Free Software"-Day 2012. If you like to know more about last years campaign, please have a look at the report of Valentine's Day 2011.

#ilovefs day 2012

As every year, Valentine's Day will be celebrated on the 14th of February, which will be a Tuesday this year. Similar to last year, the FSFE is planning an online campaign to celebrate this day as an "I love Free Software" - Day. Additionally, there will be an Offline-Event this year in Essen.

Online campaign: Spread your love for Free Software

Our online campaign aims at rising awareness to Free Software and the people behind it. Because behind every Free Software initiative and organisation are real, passionate hard-working people. Show them that you appreciate their work – it could make all the difference!
Of course, there are lots of ways to declare one's love... Here are just some ideas on how you can go public and proclaim your love to Free Software.

  • Send active Free Software supporters a message thanking them for their work. Do not underestimate the effects of such a message. As many Free Software developers work hard for their projects without earning money, a nice "thank you"/"love you" is a good currency for further encouragement
  • Especially on Valentine's Day, microblog about how you love Free Software, and use the Hashtag #ilovefs.
    • Encourage your friends to do as well, to get a high ranking on Twitter trends and hashtag timelines in identi.ca and other blogging system.
  • If you are a Blogger, blog about the "I love Free Software"-Day and the idea behind it. Get people attracted by the idea of Free Software. Look out for our Banners.
  • If you are living in Germany, please promote our Offline-Event in the Unperfekt-Haus in Essen

Banners for your website or blog

We set up a page showing different banners that support the "I love Free Software" - Day and that are free to use. Some even come along with code snippets to easily implement it on your homepage or blog. We hope you find your personal favorite.

Show your love - Individually

  • Hug a developer (Obviously better to ask permission first ; )
  • Buy your favourite developer a drink. Or buy someone else a drink and while savouring it, tell him/her about your favorite Free Software application!
  • Use your lunch break at work to talk with your colleagues about Free Software and why it is worth to support our movement.
  • Send a person a Postcard to tell her about your love to Free Software. We will hope to be able to provide you some drafts in the next days here on this webpage.
  • Send a book to your favourite contributor and support FSFE at the same time.
  • Wear shirts and sweaters showing your support for Free Software. If you need new stuff, you can order some from us, or from your favourite Free Software project.
  • Send us pictures of how you're expressing your feelings for Free Software. We'll publish them on our website, making a gallery of Free Software lovers from around the world!

Money can't buy you love...

… but it can help to fight for your freedom. Donate to FSFE or another Free Software initiative to express your gratitude. They depend on your contribution to continue their work. So check out your favourite organisation and make a donation. You can be sure they will love you back.

Offline-Event: I love Free Software

This year for the first time, there will be an "I love Free Software" - offline event, too. This event will take place at the Unperfekthaus in Essen. The Unperfekthaus in Essen seems to be a good location to celebrate Free Software, as it is a building to host free minds at nearly no cost to elaborate ideas and thougts, individual or collective - similar to many Free Software projects. We would like this event to bring together Free Software users, activists, developers and everyone else who is in favor of Free Software.

We are still in progress of setting up our schedule, but there are some hard facts, yet, about how the day will be:
When? 14/02/2012 - The whole day, our event will be situated in the Unperfekthaus
How Much? Everybody who wants to join our event has to pay normal fee for the Unperfekthaus, but is allowed - of course - to leave our event and to visit the whole Unperfekthaus like usual paying includes. (please visit the homepage to learn more about the house and its visiting possibilities)
What to expect? Over the day we will offer some workshops and introductions to Free Software and its aspects. This will give a chance for newcomers to get to know what we are talking about when we say "Free as in Free Software". But there will be some discussions and informations about ongoing projects, unperfekt ideas and so on for experienced Free Software users, too.
And my family?As we do not want to get in conflict with traditional Valentine's Day family celebration, we tried to set up the event in a way that you can bring your family, too. There will be amusement for the kids as well as amusements for loving partnerships. Please, feel free to celebrate Free Software with your whole family and bring them with you!
You like to contribute something? We will set up a speakers corner for everyone to do brief speeches, you can set up spontaneous workshops or discussion groups. For example, feel free to present your own Free Software product or the one you love the most ... In the end and over all, there is the goal to celebrate Free Software together and have some fun, to get to know each other and to learn from each other ....
Night enterntainmentIn the evening some bands will be playing live-music. These bands will be announced in next days.

Please check this page regularly for updates on what will happening during the I love Free Software Day meeting.
