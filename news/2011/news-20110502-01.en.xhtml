FSFE submits its reply to the EC on consultation on collective redress

Free Software Foundation Europe has sent its contribution to the European Commision's public consultation on identification of common legal principles on collective redress.

"Collective redress" is a legal concept where citizens and businesses, harmed by illegal behaviour of the same company, are allowed to bundle their claims in a single procedure to simplify the process and reduce costs.

Being concerned with numerous complaints over pre-installed Microsoft Windows operating system, FSFE has provided the EC with its vision of the main principles of the collective redress procedure in the EU which may substantially increase chances to obtain "Windows tax refund".
