The Freedom Task Force provides licensing services to individuals, projects and businesses involved with Free Software.

Are you looking for information about our training course?

Licence consultancy and education

We offer consultancy and education in Free Software licensing. We can answer questions, provide training and help implement best practices. We offer a variety of services for individuals, community projects and commercial businesses, using our network of legal and technical experts throughout Europe to deliver timely solutions. Learn more...

Fiduciary services

We can act as the legal guardian of Free Software projects. We can consolidate and manage the legal side of things while you focus on ensuring your project reaches its full potential. We can also help consolidate your copyright under your own administrative umbrella with the Fiduciary Licence Agreement (FLA). Learn more...

Licence enforcement

We work in partnership with gpl-violations.org to resolve licensing issues. We seek to resolve problems for the long-term benefit of Free Software rather than regarding with each case as an isolated infringement. We seek to educate people in how to properly use Free Software. Learn more...
