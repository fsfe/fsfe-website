Freedom Task Force

Licensing enforcement

The FTF works in partnership with gpl-violations.org to resolve licensing issues. We seek to resolve any problems for the long-term benefit of Free Software. This means more than dealing with each case as an isolated occurrence. We seek to educate people in how they can properly make use of Free Software.

Do you have any license enforcement questions? Do you have violations to report?
