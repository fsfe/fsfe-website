Contribute to Free Software

If you wish to contribute back to Free Software, FSFE always welcomes help from volunteers. Our work is made possible thanks to this community of people who wish to keep us going strong for Free Software. No matter what your skills are or how much time you have, there's always a way to help us! For example, you can make a small donation or with only a few minutes per week, you can help translate our website quickly.

Navigation

  • Join the Fellowship

    The Fellowship is the primary way people can support our organisation. The community of fellows contributes to our work and helps us for our campagins or on our booths.

  • Join our mailing lists

    FSFE has Europe-wide, country-specific and activity-focused mailing lists. Depending on which topic you're interested in, or in which area you live, you will find a place in this community.

  • Contribute to the website

    Our website needs to give a good impression. Help us, become a translator or become a webmaster. Take a look also on our wiki, where you generate the content.

  • Get involved with your country teams

    In many countries, FSFE has a local team. If you want to get deeper involved in FSFE's work, contact them. FSFE is trying to be actively working all across Europe, you may be interested in starting a local team yourself!

  • Spread the word!

    Spread these graphics to show the visitors of your website that you care about Free Software and FSFE!

  • Make a donation

    Your donations allow us to be autonomous and to keep going strong for Free Software. With your contribution, you lay the foundations for incorruptible and undeviating work.

  • Internship

    FSFE operates in a very international environment and it is involved in legal, political and educational activities at various levels.
