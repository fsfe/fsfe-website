Documents of the FSF Europe for the 6th Framework Programme

The European Union has launched its 6th Framework Program for research and development, that will cover Europe's funded reasearch for the period 2002-2006.

The commission issued a call for expressions of interest (EOI.FP6.2002), asking for interested parties to submit proposals for the use of the new instruments (Integrated Projects and Networks of Excellence) for the Thematic Priorities.

The FSF Europe has undertaken several tasks in order to integrate Free Software as a key element of the European Union's reasearch and development funding policy, in response to this definition process:

  • April 30th, 2002 : a general recommendation by the FSF Europe and supporting parties for the 6th European Community Framework Programme: "Free Software is a concept that has fundamentally changed the way some parts of the IT sector are working towards a more stable, lasting and sustainable approach with higher dynamics and increased efficiency. It is obvious that the first region to adopt and support this principle on a larger scale can profit enormously and get a head-start in the information age."
  • June 4th, 2002 : two formal Expressions of Interest to the Commission's call (EOI.FP6.2002):
    • FOCAL: "FOcusing Competence for Advantages of Liberty", a proposal for a Network of Excellence (HTML - PDF)
    • LAFIS : "LAying the Foundations for Information Society", a proposal for an Integrated Project (HTML - PDF)
