Fellowship Group Berlin

This is the page of a local Fellowship group of the Free Software Foundation Europe. Here you find all relevant information about who we are, what we do and how to contact us. No matter if you are already a Fellow or not - we are always happy about open minded people interested in Free Software.

Berlin Fellowship Group

About

Group Name: "Berl in Freedom"
Philosophy: "Hacking for freedom"

Founded in: 2007
Members: ~ 15
Coordinator(s): Erik & Silvan

Description:

The Fellowship group in Berlin is a group of people that love Free Software and the idea of bringing people together that care about Software freedom as well as civil digital rights. Some of us are hackers, some are scientists and others do something completely different. What brings us together is the love for Free Software and its philosophy of sharing and caring.
Our meetings usually have an Agenda, or let's say a topic, along that we discuss the use of Free Software. Sometimes we have a talk, sometimes we do certain acitivities, sometimes we support FSFE's campaigns. After the 'official' part we socialize and spend a nice evening together.
No matter if you are interested in joining our activities or only like to chat with likeminded people - you are welcome!
The meetings are open for public and you explicitly do not have to be or become a Fellow to join the meetings.

Get in contact:

To get in contact with the Berlin Fellowship group you can contact Berlins coordinators directly (see above). Or you subscribe to the mailing list and contact the whole group by writing to the mailing list.

Meetings:

We meet regularly every second Thursday a month in the youth center "E-Lok" in Laskerstraße 6-8, 10245 Berlin (see below for more information about the next meeting). Meeting time begins at 7.30pm, official start of the Agenda is 8pm. There is good food available for little money, the same is true for drinks (alcoholic and non-alcoholic). At the same time there is a meeting of the local Linux User-Group "Linux-Works!" - what leads to interesting interchanges from time to time.

The agenda of the meeting generally is tripartite:

  • Report of the most important things since the last meeting directly out of FSFE's office by its employees (15 min)
  • A topic to discuss (1 h)
  • Meta discussion about past and future events and topics
