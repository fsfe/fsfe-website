About the Workshops

In collaboration with local partners and groups the FSFE runs "Upcycling Android" workshops to help you with the technical part of upgrading your default Android phone with a Custom ROM. You can read about how this works and why it helps on our upcycle page

Since we are working together with local gorups, any workshop is unique and different. They happen in different places and locations but they also put different focus each. Some focus on installing Android alternatives other might concentrate on installing full Linux replacements. But even within these categories they might differ in the actual operating system they concentrate on installing and offer help with. Please read the desctiptions carefully to be sure you choose the workshop that matches your interests. You find however some general remarks for all workshops below.

General remarks on the workshops

If you visit on of our workshops please ensure that:

  • You bring your device, power plug, data cable and if possible also a laptop where you have the admin possibilities to install software necessary to use for flashing our phone.
  • You make a backup before. All data on your phone will be erased during the process. You can find some information about backup here.
  • Technology and hacking can be fiddly sometimes. The teams running the workshops do this voluntarily. Please bring some patience and be excellent to each other.
  • Our workshops adhere to our Code of Conduct.
