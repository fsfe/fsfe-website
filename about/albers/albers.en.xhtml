About Erik

Erik Albers is a political scientist and activist. IT affine since his youth, he is concerned about users and software freedom since many years now. After his studies, Erik did an internship in a Free Software event agency and the Free Software Foundation Europe, where he got involved in our Policy Team. Since October now, he is working for FSFE's campaigns and as a deputy Fellowship coordinator. Erik's political interest are about democratization, participation and freedom of speech. His legal interests are in copyright and patent law.

Erik is available for

  • Advice and helping hand for all Fellowship activites
  • Organising and helping hand for FSFE booths
  • Presentations and talks
  • FreeYourAndroid workshops
