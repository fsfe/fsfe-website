Free Software PDF readers

Governments should not force their citizens to use proprietary software. Neither should they demand that citizens use one application alone when comparable alternatives exist. Yet thousands of government websites do exactly this every day.

The problem

Governments the world over use PDF to share information with their citizens. To the state it offers many benefits, but messages telling users that only Adobe's software must be used are false, and serve only to restrict our freedom.

By reporting to PDFreaders.org proprietary PDF advertisements delivered by your government, we can shame public organisations into removing endorsements of proprietary software.

How to participate

From the 13th of September to the 17th October we ask you to hunt down Government websites that advertise proprietary PDF readers and report them using the form below.

The three best individual hunters will win a free entry to FSCONS, the world's premier conference for social change in technology taking place in Gothenburg from November 5th to November 7th 2010. Additionally, the winning team and the individual winners will receive a special FSFE gift box at the end of October.
