FSFE 25 - Schedule

This year, the FSFE turns 25 and we will celebrate it. On 18 and 19 July we will spend the weekend in Kraków filled with talks, networking, and plenty of fun. Below is the schedule of all the events taking place over that weekend.

The weekend talks are part of EuroPython 2026 Sprints, at Echo Miasta (Kapelanka 56, 30-347 Kraków)
