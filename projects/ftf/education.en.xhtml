We offer training and consultancy in Free Software licensing. We can answer questions, provide training and help implement best practices. We offer a variety of services for individuals, community projects and commercial businesses, using our network of legal and technical experts throughout Europe to deliver timely solutions.

Training:

We can offer individual training, training seminars or complete training courses around Free Software licensing issues. The FTF currently offers training courses in Sweden, Switzerland and The Netherlands. Our Swedish partners are Internet Academy, our Swiss partners are Digicomp Academy and our Dutch partners are ATComputing. Our goal is to help ensure the provision of high quality educational courses for individuals, projects and businesses interested in Free Software. There is detailed information about our training courses here.

Consultancy:

Shane Coughlan, FTF coordinator, is available for reserved consultancy sessions in FSFE's Zurich office. These sessions cost 150 Euro per hour, with a special discounted rate of 100 Euro for companies that have Fellowship members among their employees. The FTF can also organise and hold in-house training sessions and workshop on Free Software licensing.

Find Out More

To find out more about our training and consultancy services you can contact us directly.

The best way to contact us is through our online form or by sending an email to ftf@fsfeurope.org. Your messages will automatically enter our queue and be dealt with as quickly as possible.
