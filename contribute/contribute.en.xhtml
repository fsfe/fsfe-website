Contribute

We are a community of people who are committed to Free Software. Please join us in our work! There are many ways to do so and you will find a way that fits your interests and skills.

Navigation

  • Spread the word

    Give leaflets, stickers, or posters to your friends, colleagues, or distribute them in the public, and use our online graphics.

  • Participate in our discussions

    Most of our work is coordinated via mailing lists. Participate in the discussion, learn from others, and shape FSFE's positions.

  • Become a sustaining member

    Donations are critical to our strength and autonomy. Become a sustaining member by joining the Fellowship and enable us to continue fighting for Free Software wherever necessary.

  • Translate and Proofread

    Parts of our website are available in around 30 languages while others are not. Help us to reach as many people as possible by translating our articles, press releases, and newsletters into a language you know.

  • Meet us

    If you want to meet us in person, join us in an event taking place near you, including speeches, booths, or local Fellowship meetings.

  • Improve our website

    For many people our website is the first impression they get from us. As a webmaster you determine the effectiveness of our communication with the world.

  • Help as designer

    The FSFE and the Free Software community are often in need of designers for printed and electronic material. Join our team and see your designs spreading around the globe.

  • Become an intern

    Learn to work in a pan-European organisation operating at an international level. While working for software freedom, we deal with social, political and legal issues every day, and so will you.
