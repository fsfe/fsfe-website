APRIL has set up a working group to have Free Software classified as an intangible world cultural heritage by UNESCO (from an idea by Pierre Jarillon, President of ABUL).

Why ? This classification of Free Software as an intangible world cultural heritage gives an important weight to Free Software (a world-wide echo, important publicity, recognition and promotion of its philosophy, legal protection by the UNESCO, Anti-FUD arguments, etc). And it is not expensive to try. Freedom has everything to win here.
