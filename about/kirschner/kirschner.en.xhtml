About Matthias

Matthias is FSFE's Fellowship Coordinator and the Coordinator of the German team. He studied Politics and Management at the University of Konstanz (pre-diploma) and the University of Potsdam (diploma). His diploma thesis was about IT coordination in the Superior Federal Administration.

Matthias has been a GNU/Linux user since 1999. He likes playing around with window managers and pimping his e-mail setup.
