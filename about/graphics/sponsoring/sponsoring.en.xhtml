Sponsoring Buttons

Terms of usage

      Permission is given to donors of the FSF Europe to use the
      image of the appropriate level (information about sponsoring
      levels can be found on the donation page) in any media, 
      provided that they are not used in conjunction with advertisement 
      for proprietary software. When used on the  web or in other 
      interactive media, they should be made a link to 
       http://fsfeurope.org/help/thankgnus-2002.html
