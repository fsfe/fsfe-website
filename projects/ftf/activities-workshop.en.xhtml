Activities

The European Legal Network (ELN) Workshop is held yearly to bring together lawyers, counsel and Free Software project leaders to spread knowledge about issues at the forefront of project governance: legal, licensing, trademarks, patents and management are discussed. The programme for the workshop is provided by members of the ELN.

Venue

The ELN 2010 Workshop will be held at the Lloyd Hotel near the heart of Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Rooms can be booked there or at the nearby NH Barbizon Palace or NH Tropen.

Registration

The workshop is open to members of the ELN; other attendees please inquire. Space is limited, so register early.

Registrations may be done by email to Hugo Roy (eln-registration@fsfeurope.org).

Business ticket

As before, there is no fee to participate in this event, and costs are kindly underwritten by sponsors. This is very much appreciated.

We would like to offer attendees a way to support the facilitation of the Legal Network throughout the coming year while attending the workshop. To accomplish this we will introduce an optional "business ticket" priced at 300 Euro. Invoices for accounting purposes will be provided. All revenue generated by this ticket will contribute towards the time and other resources consumed in running network communications, projects and events throughout 2010.

If you have questions about the business ticket, please contact Hugo Roy or Adriaan de Groot.
