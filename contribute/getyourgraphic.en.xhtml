Spread the word!

You found your way here, because - like us - you care about Free Software and digital civil rights. We need your help to spread the word about the advantages of Free Software, and to explain others why they should care about Freedom, too. Advocating for Free Software is fun and arguably one of the best ways to help the Free Software movement! On this page you find graphics to use and leaflets to be printed by yourself to support and help you in spreading the word. We have a separate page if you search for professionally printed promotion material by the FSFE, available for no cost.

On this page you find

All graphics you find on this page can be used to promote Free Software in general and the work of FSFE in particular. Feel free to copy, spread and use them as long as you avoid the appearance that - whatever you use it for - is an official product or the official opinion of the FSFE. Thank you very much for promoting Freedom!
