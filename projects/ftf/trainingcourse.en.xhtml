The FTF currently offers training courses in Sweden, Switzerland and The Netherlands. Our Swedish partners are Internet Academy, our Swiss partners are Digicomp academy and our Dutch partners are ATComputing. Our goal is to help ensure the provision of high quality educational courses for individuals, projects and businesses interested in Free Software. We offer courses ranging from introducing Free Software licensing through to the strategic implementation of Free Software in business.
