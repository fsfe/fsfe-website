We offer things for free, but...

Thank you for your interest in exploring our promotion material. It seems you visited us via a freebie website offering things you can get for free. We would like to ask you to think about your reasons to order free promotion material from us.

The Free Software Foundation Europe (FSFE) is a non-profit organisation to empower users to control technology. We enjoy giving our volunteers and interested people the possibility to order promotion and information material for free to enable them to fight for Free Software.

However, as a charity we cannot afford sending out masses of promotion material for personal use only. We are not making any profit from providing stickers, posters and leaflets. Our strict privacy policy also disallows abuse of any personal information people provide in the order forms.

Therefore, we would like to ask you to rethink whether you really need such a package. For your decision, please consider the shipping and material costs this causes, as well as the ecological footprint. If you never heard of Free Software and the FSFE before, please learn more about Free Software, our work and activities first.

If you feel enthusiastic about our work, we are happy to send you information material. Please also have a look at our latest t-shirts, hoodies and merchandise and consider becoming a supporter or donor of the FSFE. Thank you!
