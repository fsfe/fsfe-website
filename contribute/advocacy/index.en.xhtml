Spread the word!

You are here because, like us, you care about Free Software. We need your help to spread the word about the advantages of using Free Software, and help convince others that they should care too. Advocating for Free Software is easy and arguably one of the best ways to help the Free Software movement.

We have graphics, banners and web buttons for you to share on your web site, and printable leaflets for you to share with your friends and other people interested in Free Software and FSFE. Either you're manning a FSFE booth, running your own blog, or convincing others why freedom in software is important, we can provide you with tools to do just that.
