6th Framework Programme Documents

Documents by the FSF Europe for the 6th European Community framework programme 2002--2006 (FP6), which will cover funding by the European Commission for research and development during the period 2002-2006.


    April 30th, 2002

    Recommendation by the FSF Europe and supporting parties for the 6th framework programme:
    "Free Software is a concept that has fundamentally changed the way some parts of the IT sector are working towards a more stable, lasting and sustainable approach with higher dynamics and increased efficiency. It is obvious that the first region to adopt and support this principle on a larger scale can profit enormously and get a head-start in the information age."
    http://fsfeurope.org/documents/fp6/recommendation.html

    Coordination mailing-list

    We have set-up a discussion list in order to help coordination in building consortia to respond to the EC calls with Free Software activities.

    If you are a strong supporter of Free Software and willing to setup or participate in a proposal for a FP6 project (IP, NoE, or other "old" instruments), to advance both your RtD activities and Free Software, please join our list at : http://mailman.fsfeurope.org/mailman/listinfo/fp6 (subscription to this mailing-list is moderated).
