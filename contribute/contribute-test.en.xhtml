Contribute

Our work is made possible by a community of people who support our commitment to Free Software. If you would like to help us achieve our goals, there are many ways to contribute - no matter what your skills or time limitations.

Navigation

  • Join the Fellowship

    Fellows secure our financial independence, increase our political weight, and support our local activities.

  • Contribute to the website

    As a webmaster or translator you determine the effectiveness of our communication with the world.

  • Spread the word

    Use these graphics to show others that you care about Free Software.

  • Internship

    Learn how to work in a pan-European organisation which is involved in social, political, and legal issues from national to UN level.

  • Join our mailing lists

    Our mailing lists make it easy for you to participate in discussions on education, design, country-specific activities, and much more.

  • Get involved with your country teams

    Find out what we are doing in your country, and get involved with like minded people in your locality.

  • Make a donation

    Your donations are critical to our strength and autonomy - they enable us to continue fighting for Free Software wherever necessary.
