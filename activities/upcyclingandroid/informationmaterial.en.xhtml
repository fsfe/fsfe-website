Our information material

Help us to spread the word about the environmental impact of our smartphones and how to tackle them by Upcycling your Android with Free Software. On this page you find printed stickers that you can order for no costs or download to spread them in the Internet. Also you find diverse infographics and pictures for you to download, share and help you to explain the cause to other people.
