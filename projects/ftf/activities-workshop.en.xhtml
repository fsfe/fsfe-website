Activities

The European Legal Network (ELN) Workshop is held yearly to bring together lawyers, counsel and Free Software project leaders to spread knowledge about issues at the forefront of project governance: legal, licensing, trademarks, patents and management are discussed. The programme for the workshop is provided by members of the ELN.

Programme

The ELN 2010 Workshop will be held on April 8 and 9, 2010. The programme has not been finalized yet. Main topics of the workshop are: GOVERNANCE, REAL-WORLD DEVELOPMENTS, LINKING, LEVERAGING BRANDS, GPLv3 AND COMPLIANCE, CONTAINING RISK, MAXIMIZING BENEFIT, EXPLORING HOW PATENTS AND SOFTWARE INNOVATION INTERSECT, LOOKING FORWARD.

Venue

The ELN 2010 Workshop will be held at the Lloyd Hotel near the heart of Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Rooms can be booked there or at the nearby NH Barbizon Palace or NH Tropen.

Registration

The workshop is open to members of the ELN; other attendees please inquire. Space is limited, so register early.

Registrations may be done by email to Hugo Roy (roy at fsfeurope.org).
