Get Involved

Would you like to raise awareness for software freedom? If you liked the book, here are some ideas.

Read it to others

The author Matthias Kirschner has already given several readings of the book: At events for children and adults (from 6 to 106 years), in school classes, libraries and on other occasions. If you are are interested in a reading by the author or in other available languages by FSFE volunteers, please contact us at contact@fsfe.org.

Share your thoughts with others

Gift the book to others

Suggest to Libraries / Schools

Include e-book and movies as example files in installations at schools, universities, etc.

Organise a Screening

Translate

The FSFE would like as many people as possible to learn about the book and being able to read it in their mother tongue. If you would like to do a community translation, please let the FSFE's translation team know about it and have a look at the translation guidelines of the Git repository. If you have any contacts with publishers, which are interested to publish the book in another language, or if you are interested to order a higher amount of books if the book would be available in a certain language, then please reach out to us .
