Free Software Foundation Europe

This is the web site of the FSF Europe, an organization dedicated to Free Software activities in Europe. As the official sister organization of the Free Software Foundation in the United States, it will build its activities around the GNU Project although it won't be limited to it.

The FSF Europe began its activity on March 10th, 2001. In the background section, you will find general information on Free Software, the Free Software Foundation and FSF Europe.

24 April 2001, two press releases : Free Software Foundation Europe finishes founding process and First general assembly of the Free Software Foundation Europe.
