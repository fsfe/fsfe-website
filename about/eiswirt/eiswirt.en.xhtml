Susanne Eiswirt

Project and Finance Manager

Susanne has studied Philosophy and Interdisciplinary Media Studies in Bielefeld and moved to Berlin in 2011 to continue and deepen her private engagement in the field of digital human rights.
She was responsible for public relations at the newthinking communications GmbH and was coordinating press relations for the re:publica Berlin in 2014 and 2015. Furthermore she has been managing the finances of the Center for the Cultivation of Technology gGmbH, a charitable non-profit host organization that takes care of organizational matters for international Free Software projects.

Voluntary, she was a board member of Digitale Gesellschaft e.V. from 2015 to 2017 and is supporting different non profit organizations with various tasks, from bylaws, applications for funding and finance to campaign and graphic work.

You can directly get in contact with Susanne via email (PGP).
