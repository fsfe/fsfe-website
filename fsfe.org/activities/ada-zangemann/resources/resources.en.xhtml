Teaching Materials

If you’re looking for teaching resources for the classroom, we’d like to provide you with a range of materials so that you can engage the children in various activities related to “Ada & Zangemann”:

  • Drawing templates
  • Video: "What is Free Software in less than 3 minutes"

Slides & Printables

Do you like "Ada & Zangemann"? If you’d like to continue supporting the book and help spread its message, we provide some "Ada & Zangemann" stickers. You can order them at no cost on our information material page. Get them all!

You can also post your review on your social media accounts using our sharepic generator.

Source Files / Git

In the Git repository you can find all the available sources and adicional material for the illustrated children's book and movie "Ada & Zangemann - A Tale of Software, Skateboards and Raspberry Ice Cream".

Online version from French Ministry

On 4 December 2023, the French Minister of Education, Gabriel Attal, presented the book “Ada & Zangemann” to the APFA parliamentarians meeting in the old Bonn parliament. Afterwards he gifted the book to Anke Rehlinger, Minister-President of the Saarland (Germany).
