Youth Hacking 4 Freedom

Welcome to this hacking competition for young people from Europe! If you like experimenting with software development, if you often start coding and find yourself losing motivation halfway, if you want to meet people of your age with the same interests, this winter just got way more exciting.

What is this competiton about?

Every participant shall submit a software project. The possibilities are endless! The only condition for the competition is that the program should be Free Software. We will show you how to label your program as Free Software during the beginning of the competition.

What can I win?

Our large cash prizes will keep you focused, and, if won, they can be used to advance your education on Computer Science, or in any other way you want. The first places for women and men will be awarded with 4.096€, the second places with 2.048€, and the third places with 1.024€. All participants who submit a program at the end of the round will get a participation certificate.

How much time do I have?

Register until 10 October 2021. The coding phase begins at 15 October 2021 and ends at 30 April 2022. Then, our jury members will evaluate all submission. You are not expected to devote all this time into your project; you have seven months so that you can be flexible in your schedule. The Award Ceremony will take place in June 2022 in Brussels (Belgium).
