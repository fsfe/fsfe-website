Information for Editors

The editors team is responsible for editing and proofreading material content published by FSFE. New articles are requested which the team provide, and other teams; particularly the web team, make extensive use of the editor's skills. Contribute your command of English to the Free Software movement by signing up to the editors' mailing list.
