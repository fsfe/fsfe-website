1 Libre software, patrimony of mankind

Why? (1)

For free software community and for mankind

  • Libre software is not just technical skills or licenses.
  • Freedom: to copy, to study, to modify, to redistribute
  • Equality: same rights for everybody
  • Fraternity: to share and help others
  • Libre software is patrimony of mankind
  • UNESCO and free software community share the same values.
