FTF

Activities

The European Legal Network Conference is held annually to bring together lawyers, counsel and Free Software project leaders to discuss the current market situation and projected future for Free Software licenses. This is the world's premier event for debating, discussing and sharing knowledge around Free Software legal affairs.

The next event will be held on the 7th and 8th April in Amsterdam.

Venue

The event will be held at the new City Inn hotel near Centraal Station, Amsterdam.

Registration

If you are interested in attending this event, please email FSFE legal at (legal at fsfeurope.org). Registration is free, though places are limited.
