FSFE Legal - The Freedom Task Force

Overview

FSFE collects and shares knowledge about Free Software law, safeguarding the interests of individuals, projects, governments and businesses that participate in promoting freedom.

Answering Questions

Everyone can contact FSFE about Free Software licensing and legal issues. While we do not provide legal advice, we do provide access to information, experts and support.

  • Email us at legal@fsfeurope.org

We accept license violation reports at this address too, and have a guide to help you format your submission.

Networking Experts

FSFE facilitates the world's largest private, vendor-neutral network of legal experts in the field of Free Software licensing. This network drives discussions and develops knowledge around licensing and legal issues, providing a unique forim for driving maturity.

  • The European Legal Network

FSFE maintains a collection of documentation on Free Software Legal and Licensing topics. It also produces guides and handbooks.
