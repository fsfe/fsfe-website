Finding support for Free Software

There are many ways to find help with Free Software applications.The Free Software support community consists of tens of thousands of volunters and hundreds of companies all over the world, in multiple timezones and languages. Knowing how to get the assistance you need is an important and easily learned skill, and the resources below are designed to get you started.
