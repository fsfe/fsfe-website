The FTF can answer questions, provide training and help individuals, projects and businesses implement best practice for using Free Software.

Training:

We offer seminars and training courses on topics ranging from introducing Free Software licensing through to the strategic implementation of Free Software in businesses. The FTF currently offers training courses in Germany, Belgium, Sweden, Switzerland and The Netherlands. Our Swedish partners are Internet Academy, our Swiss partners are Digicomp Academy and our Dutch partners are ATComputing. Our goal is to help ensure the provision of high quality educational courses for individuals, projects and businesses interested in Free Software. There is detailed information about our training programme here.

Consultancy:

We offer consultancy either at FSFE's Zurich office or at other locations through specific arrangement. These sessions cost 150 Euro per hour, with a special discounted rate of 100 Euro for companies that have Fellowship members among their employees. The FTF can also organise and hold in-house training sessions and workshops on Free Software.

Find Out More

To find out more about our training and consultancy services you can contact us directly.

The best way to contact us is through our online form or by sending an email to ftf@fsfeurope.org. Your messages will automatically enter our queue and be dealt with as quickly as possible.
