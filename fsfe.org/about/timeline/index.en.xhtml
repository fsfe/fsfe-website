FSFE Timeline - Working for Free Software since 2001

With the support of our Supporters, and our corporate donors FSFE has been promoting and defending all aspects of software freedom in Europe since 2001. Take a look at our most important achievements in different areas with our timeline.

Our work can be divided in four often intertwined categories, represented on the timeline below. "Internal" 🤝︎ presents the work accomplished to have a solid structure, carrying efficiently the rest of FSFE's actions. "Policy" 🏛︎ brings together successes brought by a continuous involvement in local, national and European law making processes. "Legal" ⚖︎ presents the achievements of the Legal team, and "Public" 📣︎ lists the largest steps of our general advocacy work and campaigns.
