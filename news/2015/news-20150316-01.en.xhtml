And the winner of the election for FSFE's Fellowship GA seat is…

Election results

… Nicolas Dietrich! The election period for this year's Fellowship GA seat has ended on March 13. FSFE is proud to have had three outstanding candidates running for office this time. Now, Nicolas Dietrich is happy to take over the GA's Fellowship seat from former representative Heiki "Repentinus" Löhmus.

We had a voter participation of 21,35% this time. Thanks to all Fellows who participated in the whole process and made this a valuable experience for the Fellowship and for FSFE.

Please see FSFE's wiki for more background about 2015's candidates and detailed Fellowship elections results.
