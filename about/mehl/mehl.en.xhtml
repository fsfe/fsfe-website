Team

Max Mehl

Max started to get active in FSFE in 2011 when he became member of the translators' team. Within the scope of his Bachelor in Politics and Public Administration in University of Konstanz he was FSFE's Assistant to the President for 6 months starting from October 2013. During this time he worked on several of FSFE's campaigns and activities. In August 2015 he became coordinator of the German FSFE team together with Björn Schießle. His main area is policy work and public awareness in Germany and EU. Since October 2015 he is part-time employed with FSFE.

Beside his master's studies in Politics in Münster (NRW/Germany), Max is self-employed in the IT area. After he finished his bachelor's degree he went to Tanzania from March until July 2015 as a volunteer at TAREO. From time to time he gives an update about his current activities on his blog.
