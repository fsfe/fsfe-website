Contribute

Spread the word!

You found your way here, because - like us - you care about Free Software and digital civil rights. We need your help to spread the word about the advantages of Free Software, and to explain others why they should care about Freedom, too. Advocating for Free Software is fun and arguably one of the best ways to help the Free Software movement! On this page you find a lot of support that helps you: graphics to use, leaflets to print and promotion material to order.

Logo of the Fellowship of FSFE

This is the logo that represents the Fellowship of the FSFE. Its name is 'Plussy' and the symbol illustrates a human who stands strong for his freedom. At the same time, the human figure opens his arms to include everyone who shares common goals. Finally, the symbol draws a 'plus', because our movement gets stronger with every single identity. Hence, every one who is joining the Fellowship is a plus in the fight for freedom. That is why it is called 'Plussy'.

Fellowship Plussy Logo: PNG | SVG

License: The Fellowship logo (and variations of it) can be used to advertise the Fellowship of the FSFE and in association with any Free Software related activity performed by FSFE Fellows. It can also be incorperated into logos of local Fellowship groups if so desired. However, if you do not have a mandate by FSFE, we ask you not to create the impression that you speak officially for FSFE, or that FSFE has certified or endorsed you to do so in any way.
