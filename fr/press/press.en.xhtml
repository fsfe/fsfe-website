Relations with the Press

Don't hesitate to contact us if you're a journalist. In order to contact someone specific or in charge of some task, please see the contact section.

For contacts specifi to France, please use the address (in english or french) press@france.fsfeurope.org

For contacts concerning FSF Europe, please go to its press section.

To be informed of our press releases, you may subscribe to the different following mailing-lists :

  • press-release (articles in english) ( web interface, archive)

  • press-release-fr (articles in french) ( web interface, archives)

  • press-release-de (articles in german) ( web interface, archives)
