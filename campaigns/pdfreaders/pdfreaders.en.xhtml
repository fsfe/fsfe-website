Free Software PDF Readers

What would you think about a sign on the highway saying “You need a Volkswagen to drive on this road. Contact your Volkswagen dealer for a gratis test drive – Your Government”? Governments do exactly this with PDF reader software, every day, all around the world.

With the pdfreaders.org campaign, we will turn the spotlight on governments, showing how frequently they advertise non-free software. Once we have their attention, we will help them to better serve all of us by replacing those endorsements of a single vendor with links to Free Software PDF readers.

You can also sign our Petition For The Removal Of Proprietary Software Advertising On Government Websites by ticking the box below.

The three best individual hunters will win a free entry to FSCONS, the world's premier conference for social change in technology taking place in Gothenburg from November 5th to November 7th 2010. Additionally, the winning team and the individual winners will receive a special FSFE gift box.
