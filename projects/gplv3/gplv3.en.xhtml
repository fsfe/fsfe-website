GPLv3: Drafting version 3 of the GNU General Public License

FSFE aims to raise awareness of the proposed licence changes, to help people to participate in the public consultation, and to constantly document the ongoing discussion to make this topic as accessible as possible.

Much of FSFE's GPLv3 work is made possible by the Fellowship of FSFE. To support the this work, consider joining the Fellowship and encouraging others to do so. You can also support FSFE by making a donation.

The aim of all versions of the GPL are to ensure that everyone who receives a copy of GPL-licensed software is free to use that software, to modify it, and to redistribute it in modified or unmodified form. Version 3 will be updated to account for changes in software's legal and technical environment, and to thwart new techniques devised to take those freedoms away from software users.

How to participate

The public consultation is being managed by our sister organisation at the official GPLv3 website. There you can:

  • View existing comments
  • Sign-up for an account
  • Log-in
  • Submit your comments (after logging in)

By signing up, you will also be added to the info-gplv3 announcement mailing list and will receive infrequent notifications of key GPLv3 events.
