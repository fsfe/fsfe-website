Activities in Berlin for #ilovefs 2017

For the "I love Free Software" day 2017 Free Software enthusiasts in Berlin carried out two activities to draw attention to the importance of Free Software. They illuminated symbolic places in the city with a Gobo projector and they helped to send letters with a rose attached to all members of German parliament.

On this page you find a selection of images of both activities under CC BY-SA license. You can also read a copy of the letter we sent.
