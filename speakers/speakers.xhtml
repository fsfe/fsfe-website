Free Software and FSF Europe speakers

If you wish that a speaker who can represent the FSF Europe and/or the Free Software Movement at a conference, event or panel discussion, you can use this page to find someone to speak on behalf of the FSF Europe and/or the Free Software Movement. Speakers are listed in alphabetical order by last name. If you would like to invite one of these speakers to an event, please contact the speaker directly via email or contact us.

Speakers

[ Frédéric Couchet | Loïc Dachary | Georg C. F. Greve ]

Frédéric Couchet <FredericCouchet@fsfeurope.org>

Biographie

Frédéric Couchet is the founding president of APRIL and works in a Free Software company. He is also the president of the FSF Europe - Chapter France (aka FSFE France).

Topics
Frédéric Couchet can address the following topics :
  • Presentation of the history and concepts of the Free Software.
  • Presentation and role of APRIL
  • Operation, actions and future of the Free Software Foundation Europe
  • Software patents
Langues
French

Loïc Dachary <LoicDachary@fsfeurope.org>

Biography

Loïc met the Free Software in 1987, and is a senior developer. He contributed to many software, and is the leader of Senga (group of development of free solutions for the data processing). He is also founder member of the FSF Europe - Chapter France (aka FSFE France) and vice-président of FSF Europe.

Topics

Loïc Dachary can address the following topics :

  • Development of Free Software
  • Actions and future of FSF Europe
  • The infrastructure and dynamics of the GNU Project
  • Free solutions for the data processing
Langues
French, english

Georg C. F. Greve <GeorgGreve@fsfeurope.org>

Biography
Georg Greve is a Physicist (German Diplom from the University of Hamburg) with experience in the medical science, physical oceanography, biophysics and nanotechnology areas although computer science has always been a focal point. He is (among other things) author of the monthly GNU forum "Brave GNU World" and President of the FSF Europe. More information available at http://www.gnu.org/people/greve/.

Topics

Georg C. F. Greve can speak about the following topics:

  • The Free Software Foundation Europe
  • History, background and philosophy of the GNU Project and Free Software
  • Commercial and political aspects of the GNU Project and Free Software
  • The Brave GNU World
Langues
German (native), English (near-native)
