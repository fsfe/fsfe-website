FSFE Timeline - Working for Free Software since 2001

With the support of our Supporters, and our corporate donors FSFE has been promoting and defending all aspects of software freedom in Europe since 2001. Take a look at our most important achievements in different areas with our timeline.

Our work can be divided in five often intertwined categories, represented on the timeline below.

Device Neutrality 🕻
Presents the work of ensuring non discrimiation for devices and services.
Legal Education ⚖︎
Activities relating to legal disputes and education on Free Software legislation.
Public Money? Public Code! 🏛︎
The work of ensuring support for and use of Free Software in government settings.
Volunteer Engagement 🤝︎
Reflects the organisation and work of the FSFE's backbone, the volunteers.
Young Generation 📣︎
The FSFE's outreach and work with the next generation.
