Watch our video

If only one third of smartphone users would extend the usage lifetime of their smarphones for one year more, we could save hundreds of millions of smartphones from being produced, thus save a lot of our planet's limited resources. We have created a short video that explains this issue in a nutshell and that you can share with your friends.
