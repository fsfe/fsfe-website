Mailing Lists

Press Release Mailing Lists

Information for the press are located in our press section.

These three mailing lists are post-only by the FSF Europe and do only contain press releases. The subscriber lists are non-public.

  • press-release (for English press releases) (web interface, archive)

  • press-release-fr (for French press releases) (web interface, archive)

  • press-release-de (for German press releases) (web interface, archive)
