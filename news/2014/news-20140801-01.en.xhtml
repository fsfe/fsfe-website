Your input needed for the FSFE's strategy process

Your ongoing support of our mission makes you an important part of FSFE. As we are currently running a strategy process inside the FSFE we would love to have your feedback about our work, about what you like and maybe not like, and where we might improve.

For this, we have compiled a survey with open questions about our work, and a brief part about your background. The background is being asked, to have better picture about who you are and---since you are FSFE---who FSFE is.

All of the questions are voluntary and will only be used internally for the purpose of our evaluation. You can fill out one, two or all of them. You can answer anonymously, but we encourage you to leave us a email-address, so we can contact you if we have further questions about your answers.

The survey is available here.

We are looking forward to read your answers!
