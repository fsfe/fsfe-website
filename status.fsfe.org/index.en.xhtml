Delivery of e-mails on our mailing lists is delayed. The issue could be pined to AI training harvester crawlers that crawled our public mailing list archives. For a while, the delay went down from several days to around 2 hours, but it is back up to around 2 days.

Copying data to the new server is taking longer than expected, so we currently cannot provide an exact time frame for the fix, but we will continue to work on it and we will update you here.
