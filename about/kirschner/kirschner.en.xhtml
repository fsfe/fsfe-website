About

Matthias is FSFE's Fellowship Coordinator and the Deputy Coordinator of the German team. He studied Politics and Management at the University of Konstanz (pre-diploma) and the University of Potsdam (diploma). His diploma thesis was about "IT coordination in the Superior Federal Administration".

Matthias is using GNU/Linux since 1999. He likes playing around with window managers and pimping his e-mail setup.
