News from the Free Software world in France and FSF France activities

News also available by daily mail delivery, or RSS feed for inclusion in your web site.

&news;

The news published by FSF France are dispatched on a daily basis by mail on the fsfe-newsfr-en@gnu.org mailing-list. To subscribe to this mailing-list, send a mail to fsfe-newsfr-en-request@gnu.org with the subject "subscribe".

To display these news on your web site use the RSS Channel and copy the XSL file that transforms it into HTML or modify it according to your taste.
