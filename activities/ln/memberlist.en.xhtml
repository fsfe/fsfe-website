Update Legal Network Membership Information

On this page, you can add or update information about yourself. This will compose a document that is available for all Legal Network members in order to gain an oversight of all members, find contact details, and learn about specialists in different professions and legislative systems. The information will be compiled by the Legal Network coordinators regularly.

In order to update information about yourself, you can just write the new desired text for the affected fields. The other fields will not be touched. If you wish to completely remove yourself from the document, or wish to have certain fields cleared, please contact us directly.

Please enter the email address your are subscribed with on the Legal Network mailing list. Only if these match, we can successfully add/update your information.
Optional: shortly describe yourself. 280 characters maximum.
Optional: in which countries are you professionally active, e.g. available for hire? Please use English names, and separate by comma. Example: Spain, USA, Japan, Great Britain
Optional: one link to further info about yourself, e.g. homepage or social media site.
