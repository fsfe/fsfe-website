Freedom Task Force

We provide licence support services for Free Software. These services include education, fiduciary activities, protection and guidance for individuals, projects and businesses.

Goals

  • To help build, analyse and share legal knowledge about Free Software.
  • To safeguard the interests of Free Software projects and businesses.
  • To bring together experts in Free Software legal issues.
  • To work with other groups and help them achieve similar goals.

Methods

The FTF operates with a network of legal and technical volunteers with a full-time coordinator. On demand, it will form ad-hoc teams of technical experts and lawyers to deal with specific issues.

The FTF believes in positive engagement and constructive dialogue. It seeks to resolve issues amicably and for the long-term benefit of Free Software.

The FTF works in partnership with gpl-violations.org.

Services

  • Licensing education
  • Fiduciary services
  • Licence enforcement
