Teaching Materials

If you’re looking for teaching resources for the classroom, we’d like to provide you with a range of materials so that you can engage the children in various activities related to “Ada & Zangemann”:

  • Drawing templates
  • Letters to Zangemann
  • Video: "What is Free Software in less than 3 minutes"

Slides & Printables

If you need illustrations of the book, you can check this directory, which contains the illustrations, including the drawn titles and drawn capitals.

We also offer generated slides for presentations with texts for book readings.

As promotion material, we provide some "Ada & Zangemann" stickers. You can order them at no cost on our information material page. Get them all!

Source Files / Git

In the Git repository you can find all the available sources and adicional material for the illustrated children's book and movie "Ada & Zangemann - A Tale of Software, Skateboards and Raspberry Ice Cream".

Online version from French Ministry

On 4 December 2023, the French Minister of Education, Gabriel Attal, presented the book “Ada & Zangemann” to the APFA parliamentarians meeting in the old Bonn parliament. Afterwards he gifted the book to Anke Rehlinger, Minister-President of the Saarland (Germany).

Mailing list for educators using materials

You would like to stay informed about the materials for educators or receive general news about "Ada & Zangemann"? You can subscribe to the FSFE's mailings and not miss this information as well as also receiving general information about software freedom and the FSFE's work.
