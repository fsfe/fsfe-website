The Freedom Task Force coordinates European Legal and Technical Networks, with each participant being referred to as a 'delegate' of their respective network. Both of these networks are invitation only, though if you are interested in participating you can contact us.

The Legal Network:

The European Legal Network is an informal network of lawyers and legal experts. Our goal is to strengthen the legal foundation of Free Software through building connections between professionals and researchers active on the continent. The network currently has over 40 legal experts, covers sixteen European countries and maintains contacts in Canada, the USA, Singapore and Taiwan.

The Technical Network:

The European Techical Network is an informal network of technical experts. It complements the European Legal Network and provides connections between professionals and researchers active on the continent. The network currently has 25 technical specialists and includes various European and Asian representatives.
