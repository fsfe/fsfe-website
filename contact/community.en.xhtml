Public Discussion

FSFE provides some infrastructure to foster public discussion on Free Software topics. Everbody is encouraged to state his own opions there following the free speech paradigm. So positions stated in the public discussion are not official positions of FSFE. For official statements see our press release lists or contact us on European or local level.
