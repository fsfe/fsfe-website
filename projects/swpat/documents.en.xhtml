Documents

These documents explain how software patents will influence very different areas of society and economy.

  • January 3rd, 2005: "Software patents are bad for your health"
    Open letter to Mr. Lee, General Secretary of the World Health Organisation (WHO) by Georg Greve

  • December 6th, 2004: " Software patents detrimental to European power supply business"
    Open letter to Mr. Haider, President of the Union of the Electricity Industry - EURELECTRIC by Georg Greve

  • November 1st, 2004: " Firefighting with petrol"
    Open letter to Mr. Barroso by Georg Greve

  • October 4th, 2004: "Allianz Group will suffer because of software patents"
    Open letter to Mr. Diekmann, CEO of Allianz Group by Georg Greve

  • September 6th, 2004: "No software patents, conserve competitive advantage!"
    Open letter to the Dutch Prime Minister Dr. Balkenende by Georg Greve

  • August 2nd, 2004: "Software patents will destroy the foundations of IBM's future"
    Open letter to Dr. Wladawsky-Berger of IBM by Stefano Maffulli and Georg Greve

  • July 6th, 2004: "Open Letter to the Fraunhofer Gesellschaft (FhG)"
    Open letter to Prof. Bullinger by Georg Greve

  • May 31st, 2004: "Open letter to Prof. Köhler on software patents"
    Open letter to Prof. Köhler, designated German President by Georg Greve

  • May 10th, 2004: "Open letter to all citizens of Europe"
    Open letter by Georg Greve and Hartmut Pilch
