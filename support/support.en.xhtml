Become a supporter of FSFE

Free Software is important to you and you are convinced or interested in its ethical and political aspects? You believe in Free Software as a precondition to a Free Society? You like the Free Software Foundation Europe and its activities?
If you approve one or more of these questions, then you can sign up now as a supporter of the FSFE to help us spread the word.

To sign up as a supporter, you just need to fill out the form below. Decide for yourself, if you like to use your real name or not, but in any case you can be sure that your privacy is important to us! That means: We will never share your data with anyone outside FSFE.

As a supporter you receive our monthly newsletter as well as occasional special news about our campaigns or other important activities of the FSFE. Feel free to use FSFE's supporter logos to show your affiliation. You are also invited to discuss recent news or general questions about Free Software and the FSFE on our general discussion list or in one of our specific country lists - being a supporter or not.

After signing up as a supporter you will receive an email to approve your subscription. Please check your inbox after subscribing and use the the included link to do so.
