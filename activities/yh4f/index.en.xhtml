Youth Hacking 4 Freedom

If you like coding, tinkering, and having fun with software and if you are up for a challenge, we have something exciting for you: "Youth Hacking 4 Freedom" (YH4F), the FSFE's hacking competition for young people from Europe!

Who can participate?

You are born between 01.01.2002 and 31.12.2008? You are up to hack on a software project of your choice? You want to get the chance to meet like-minded people, get one of our cash awards and be invited to Brussels to meet the other winners and great people from the Free Software movement? Then register until 10 October 2021.

We are dedicated to offering an inclusive environment in the YH4F, and we would like to encourage teenagers of all genders to join! We will make sure everyone will enjoy the process and will leave this competition with added knowledge and a smile.

Decide yourself what you code

The possibilities are endless! The only condition for the competition is that the program should be Free Software. We will show you how to label your program as Free Software at the beginning of the competition.

How much time do I have?

Register until 10 October 2021. After the kick-off event in October the coding phase begins at 15 October 2021 and ends at 30 April 2022. Then, our jury members will evaluate all submission. You are not expected to devote all this time into your project; you have seven months so that you can be flexible in your schedule. The Award Ceremony will take place in June 2022 in Brussels (Belgium).

Who will evaluate the projects?

From May on the evaluation phase is carried out by Free Software experts. Our jury members excel in software development and are deeply involved in the Free Software movement. Based on the jury’s grades, the winners are determined and announced.

What is the award?

All of you should have fun during the competition, have the chance to briefly follow each other’s work, to exchange ideas, and to share problem-solving tips. Everyone who in the end submits a Free Software program will get a participation certificate. The first six places will be awarded with cash between 1.024€ and 4.096€ each and all winners will be invited to Brussels for the award ceremony to meet like-minded people in June 2022.
