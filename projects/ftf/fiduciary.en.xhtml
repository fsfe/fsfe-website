Fiduciary services

The FTF can act as the legal guardian of Free Software projects. Through the Fiduciary License Agreement (FLA) we can consolidate and manage the legal side of things while developers focus on making sure projects accomplish their full potential.

The details of our selection process, our policies and our license are available for your review, though our approach can be summarised in a sentence. The FTF makes sure projects are in a strong legal position without being prescriptive; we don't dictate project policy or project management. Our job is to make sure you can do your job.
