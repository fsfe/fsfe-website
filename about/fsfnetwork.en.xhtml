The FSF* network

FSFE is one of four Free Software organizations in the world.

The following organisations are sister organisations of the FSFE in the global FSF network. We are working together on Free Software issues, each one with its own cultural background. The FSF's are legally, financially and personally independent from each other, and like every network, the FSF* network is formed of interconnected components.

  • 1985: Shortly after the GNU project the Free Software Foundation (FSF) was founded as the first organisation which cared about Free Software.
  • 2001: Free Software Foundation Europe (FSFE) was founded as the second FSF.
  • 2003: Free Software Foundation India (FSFI) founded.
  • 2005: Free Software Foundation Latin America (FSFLA) founded.
