This agenda lists events that involve a relation between the european or government officials and the european citizens on the subject of Software Patents. It deliberatly omits events such as meetings between european citizens, reports published by organizations but not sent to governmental agencies, public statements by european citizens that were not sent to governmental agencies. This decision was made to keep the agenda short and focused so that someone can quickly find out what happened on the subject of Software Patents and what are the upcoming milestones.
