Activities

The FTF builds and supports legal infrastructure for Free Software in Europe.

European Legal Network

We facilitate the European Legal Network, a private network of legal professionals with over 200 members across 27 countries and four continents. Members share knowledge across national borders and cooperate to increase the availability of information related to best practice in Free Software licensing.

Special Interest Groups

We organize special interest groups to discuss topics like business processes and the supply chain, software patents and code management in the embedded industry. These groups are designed to reduce misunderstandings, share knowledge and address aress where there is a lack of available guidance.

Conferences

We host a yearly legal conference called the 'European Legal and Licensing Workshop' to discuss the current market situation and projected future for Free Software licenses. The next European Legal and Licensing Workshop is scheduled to take place in Amsterdam, The Netherlands, on the 8th and 9th of April 2010.
