Free Software Foundation Europe at GNU/LinuxTag 2006

"Free as in Freedom"

This is the Free Software Foundation Europe's central event page for activities related to the GNU/Linuxtag 2006 in Wiesbaden, Germany.

There will be one FSFE booth: stand 3 (exhibition plan).

At the booth FSFE merchandise will be sold. There will be Fellowship-themed articles, such as lanyards and stickers hot off the printing press. Of course, a wide range of T-Shirts and other clothing will be available. All proceeds will go towards funding the Free Software Foundation Europe's work.
