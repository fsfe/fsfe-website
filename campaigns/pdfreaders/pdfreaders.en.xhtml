Free Software PDF readers

The FSFE is launching a campaign to sensibilize the public to the fact that government websites are advertising for proprietary software, each time that they redirect you to a downloading interface for a non-free PDFreader while you have to read a PDF file.

How to participate

From the 13th of September to the 17th October we propose you to search for such advertising on the governmental websites and to notify them to us thanks to the formular underneath.

You'll find on this page some screenshots to highlight want we want fo be removed from the institutional website, in order to reach advertising-free institutional websites.Click on them to enlarge the screenshots.

The ones would will notify us the most websites will win a price [SPECIFY WHAT PRICE] at the end of October.

Thank you for contributing with the FSFE to advertisment-free goverment websites

Report an institution

Remark: You can participate to the hunt as an individual participant or as a member of a group. In the case of a group, please elect a group coordinator and register your group in sending a mail to pdfreaders@lists.fsfe.org
