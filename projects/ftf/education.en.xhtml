The FTF can answer questions, provide training and help individuals, projects and businesses implement best practice for using Free Software.

Training:

We offer training courses in Germany, Belgium, Sweden, Switzerland and The Netherlands. Our Swedish partners are Internet Academy, our Swiss partners are Digicomp academy and our Dutch partners are ATComputing. Our goal is to help ensure the provision of high quality educational courses for individuals, projects and businesses interested in Free Software. We offer courses ranging from introducing Free Software licensing through to the strategic implementation of Free Software in business.

Consultancy:

We offer consultancy at FSFE's Zurich office at a flat rate of 150 Euro per hour, with a special rate of 100 Euro for companies with Fellowship members among their employees. The FTF also offers consultancy, in-house training and workshops at other locations on request.
