The FSF Europe Team

These people are working for and with the FSF Europe on a regular basis, so they have been given permanent responsibilities and authorities for certain areas. Some of them are official (voting) members, some are not.

  • Antonella Beccaria - Italian team - Italian press office coordination

    Volker Dormeyer is often put in charge of the FSF Europe booth at events like tradeshows and has organised many FSF Europe booths at tradeshows and fairs.
