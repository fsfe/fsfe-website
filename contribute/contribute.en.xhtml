Get involved!

The different ways to get involved and help FSFE:

Join the Fellowship!
The Fellowship of FSFE is the primary way people can identify with and support our organisation. Fellows add visible support and contribution to all activities of the FSFE, they get in touch with other like-minded people, and become active both in local and in international events. Are you interested? Join the Fellowship today.
Discuss about Free Software
FSFE has Europe-wide, country-specific and activity-focused mailing lists. Join the discussions, meet other members of the Free Software community, and contribute your own experiences and opinions!
Cooperate with country teams
In many countries, FSFE has a local team. If you want to get deeper involved in FSFE's work, contact the team in your country and join their activities!
Become a webmaster!
We are constantly trying to keep our content easily accessible and up-to-date. The maintenance of the website is a crucial activity for FSFE. Please consider becoming one of our webmasters!
Tranlsate our content into your own language
We try to distribute our documents, press releases, leaflets and web pages in as many languages as possible. In order to do so, we managed to built an excellent team of translators, where volunteers cooperate to translate and proofread this content into their own language. Here you find all the information you need to join the team and get started with some translations!
Join our tradeshow booths!
FSFE participates in many European Free Software events during the whole year, and at these events we always try to have a booth where we talk about Free Software, our organisation and sell our merchandise. If you like participating at fairs, talking to people and explaining the philosophy of Free Software, then read here about booth activities and get in contact with the other volunteers.
Become an intern
Are you looking for an interesting organisation where to make an internship? Then you found it! FSFE operates in a very international environment and it is involved in legal, political and educational activities at various levels. Have a look at the internship page and consider becoming on of FSFE's next interns!
Support FSFE financially!
Your donors allow us to do our work and to do it autonomously without the need to negotiate our goals: We work for Free Software principles. If you share our cause please consider donating to FSFE!
Spread FSFE's banners!
Whether you have a corporate website or a personal blog, whether you discuss digital freedoms or free software development you will always help us by spreading our banners! Use these graphics to show the visitors of your website that you care about Free Software and FSFE!
