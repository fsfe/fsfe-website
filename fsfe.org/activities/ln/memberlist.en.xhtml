Update Legal Network Membership Information

On this page, you can add or update information about yourself for the Legal Network. The information that you provide here will be included in a document that is available for all Legal Network members, to allow for an oversight of all members willing to share such information, find contact details, and learn about specialists in different professions and legislative systems. This information will be compiled by the Legal Network coordinators regularly.

In order to update information about yourself, you can just write the new desired text for the affected fields. The other fields will not be touched. If you wish to completely remove yourself from the document, or wish to have certain fields cleared, please contact us directly.

Please enter the email address you are subscribed with on the Legal Network mailing list. We can only successfully add/update your information if these match.
Optional: shortly describe yourself. 280 characters maximum.
Optional: in which countries are you professionally active, e.g. available to work in? Please use English names, and separate by comma. Example: Spain, USA, Japan, Great Britain
Optional: one link to further info about yourself, e.g. homepage or social media site.
