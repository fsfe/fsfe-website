Welcome to the home page of the Free Software Foundation Europe. The FSF Europe was launched March 10th 2001 and works towards all European aspects of Free Software and especially the GNU Project. It is actively supporting development of Free Software and furthering GNU-based systems such as GNU/Linux. Also it provides a competence center for politicians, lawyers and journalists in order to secure the legal, political and social future of Free Software.
