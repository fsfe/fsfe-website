Donations to the FSFE

The Free Software Foundation Europe is a non-profit non-governmental organisation. Our work is made possible by a community of volunteers, Fellows and donors. Your donations are critical to our strength and autonomy — they enable us to continue fighting for Free Software wherever necessary, and to be an independent voice.

Categories

  • Donate more than EUR 500 every month OR EUR 6000 in a year and you'll become a Patron of the FSFE.
  • Donate more than EUR 100 every month OR EUR 1200 in a year and you'll become a Sustaining Contributor of the FSFE.
  • Donate more than EUR 50 every month OR EUR 600 in a year and you'll become a Contributor of the FSFE.
  • Donate more than EUR 10 every month OR EUR 120 in a year and you'll become a Supporter of the FSFE.
  • Donate less than EUR 10 every month OR EUR 120 is of course also possible. Or as an individual support us by joining the Fellowship (minimum amount 60 EUR a year).

Further information

No matter which way you choose, please send an email to <office@fsfeurope.org> and let us know where to send the receipt and thank-you letter. We would like to list you on "our donors" page. Even if you prefer to remain anonymous, please let us know how to reach you, so we can thank you privately.

To find out the best way for your donation to reach us, if you have questions about different kind of donations (like bequeathals) please contact our office.
