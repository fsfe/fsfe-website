Permanent Volunteers

These people are working for and with the FSF Europe on a regular basis, so they have been given permanent responsibilities and authorities for certain areas.

  • Volker Dormeyer - Tradeshows & Events
    Volker Dormeyer is often put in charge of the FSF Europe booth at events like tradeshows and has organised many FSF Europe booths at tradeshows and fairs.

  • Martin Gerwinski - Office
    Email: martin.gerwinski@office.fsfeurope.org
    Martin Gerwinski is working together with Kai Keller in the FSF Europe office. He contributes partially on time sponsored by his company and partially in his own time.

  • Kai Keller - Office
    Email: kai.keller@office.fsfeurope.org
    Kai Keller is working together with Martin Gerwinski in the FSF Europe office. He does this partially on time sponsored by his company and partially on his own time.

  • Christian Selig - Education Activities
    Christian Selig has volunteered to coordinate the activities of the FSF Europe in the educational area.

  • Cristian Rigamonti - Webmaster and translator english-to-italian
    Cristian mantains italian version of FSF Europe's web site. He also translates from english and works in coordination with the press office and other webmasters.

All of these volunteers do an invaluable job. They and all the other volunteers are an essential part of the FSF Europe, and without them it would not be the same.
