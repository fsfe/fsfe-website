Picnic in Slottsskogen, Gothenburg, Sweden

On the 15th of May, the Free Software Foundation Europe invites you to a picnic in Slottsskogen in Gothenburg, Sweden. We will gather at Linnéplatsen at 18.30 and will then take a walk into Slottsskogen.

Discussions on Free Software, copyright, patents and other closely related areas will be in the air during the evening. Key signing will also take place, so remember to bring your GPG fingerprint and ID.

You should bring with you what you wish to eat and drink. If you wish to take part of a joint purchase of food, you can contact Jonas Öberg <oberg@fsfeurope.org> who will organise this in return for a donation to the FSF Europe.

If the weather takes a turn for the worse, we will have to gather someplace else. We will inform about that in this page before the actual picnic.
